## Operational notes

- **Send each email manually**, even if it feels slow. 12 attendees × 4–5 touch-points = ~50 sends. Worth doing one at a time. Personalization is the entire point.
- **Track every send** in your Founder Dinner sheet (in the campaign tracker spreadsheet). Columns: invited, confirmed, reminded, attended, followed-up, outcome.
- **Dietary handling:** the day before the dinner, send the venue your final list of dietary preferences. Don't show up and discover three vegetarians.
- **If you over-fill** (more than 12 RSVPs): immediately text the 13th+ person: "Quick heads-up — we hit our 12 today. I'm holding you as standby. If anything opens up I'll text you Thursday morning." Most will graciously accept the standby; a few will be relieved.
- **If you under-fill** (fewer than 8 confirmed by Tue May 26): on Tuesday morning, send a personal follow-up to anyone who hasn't responded yet — by **text, not email**. Subject line of email at this point gets ignored. SMS gets read.

Each of the 12 seats has, when guests arrive:

- **Founding 25 PDF** (printed, 1 copy)

- **One blank notecard** (for them to write you a note if they want — and ~3 of 12 always do)
- **A simple Vegas pen** (cheap is fine — just consistent across all 12 seats)
