Who qualifies

Education-sector Businesses include any Business where 60%+ of customers are connected to a school, college, or university. Verification required.

✓

Café near a college campus

Café within ½ mile of a 2-year, 4-year, or graduate school where students make up the majority of customers.

✓

University bookstore (independent)

Bookstores serving universities. Independent operators only — Barnes & Noble College / Follett don't qualify.

✓

Tutoring centers

K-12 or college tutoring services. Sylvan, Mathnasium franchises included. Independent tutors qualify.

✓

School cafeterias / food service

Independent food service operators in K-12, community college, university dining halls. Sodexo / Aramark contracts excluded.

✓

Student-housing-adjacent retail

Convenience stores, dry cleaners, salons within ½ mile of dorms or student housing.

✓

Educational supplies + equipment

Art supplies, music stores, lab equipment retailers serving school populations.

✗

National chains

Starbucks, Chipotle, Subway near campus don't qualify — even if their location serves students. Discount is for independent operators only.

✗

Non-Business educational institutions

The school itself isn't a Business that joins LYMX. We work with the Business serving the school's students.
