A note from the founder

This is the first transparency report we'll publish. We're committing to do this every year — even when the numbers are unflattering, even when no regulator asks.

Why: because we're asking customers, Businesses, and Partners to trust us with their loyalty data, their cash, and (in the Partner case) parts of their livelihood. That trust starts with showing the work.

2026 was our launch year. We're still small. The numbers below reflect that. Many sections you'd find in a mature company's transparency report — government-data requests, takedown notices, payment disputes — are short or empty. That's expected for our scale.

I'm reachable directly: kenny@getlymx.com.

— Kenny Lin, founder
