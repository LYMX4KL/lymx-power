Remote work address

If you work remotely full-time (not just an occasional WFH day), submit your home work address here. HR will set up an ongoing geofence so you can clock in / out from your home every day. You only need to do this once.

HR will geocode this address and set a 100m geofence by default. They may contact you to confirm.
Helps HR understand when to expect your daily clock-ins.
How this works
  • You submit your home address. HR reviews + sets a geofence radius.
  • Once approved, you can clock in / out from your home daily.
  • If you're working from a different location (one-off), use the single-day exception form instead.
  • If you move, just submit a new address — HR will replace the old one.
