Voice & tone

How LYMX sounds. Confident, plain-spoken, allergic to jargon. Treat customers and Businesses like adults — give them the math, then get out of the way.

✓ Do say
  • "1 LYMX = $0.01, always." Specific math.
  • "Earn at one Business, spend at any other in the network." Concrete.
  • "We don't sell your data." Direct.
  • "Most Partners earn substantially less than the maximum projected." Honest about the variance.
  • "3 spots open in the Launch 25." Real numbers.
✗ Don't say
  • "Revolutionary", "disruptive", "game-changer" — empty marketing words.
  • "Easy passive income" — never. We're FTC-careful.
  • "World-class", "best-in-class" — meaningless adjectives.
  • "Earn rewards points!" — call them LYMX. Use the actual word.
  • "Your data is safe with us" without specifics — vague trust language is a red flag.
