100 words
LYMX is a shared loyalty network giving independent local Businesses the network-effect leverage that chain stores have always had. Customers earn LYMX promotional credit units at any participating Business and spend them at any other, at a fixed face value of $0.01 per unit. Each new Business brings their existing customer base into the network — roughly 1,500 new customers per Business per month — turning customer acquisition into a structural byproduct of Business signups. Founded in 2026 in Las Vegas, Nevada, LYMX is launching with 25 founding Businesses in its Q3 2026 pilot. Pre-seed stage.

Founder bio

Permission granted to use as-is or trim.

KL

Kenny Lin

Founder & CEO, LYMX

Kenny Lin is the founder and CEO of LYMX, a shared loyalty network for independent local Businesses launching in Las Vegas in Q3 2026. Before LYMX, Kenny spent a decade operating independent Businesses across multiple categories and watching the same pattern repeat: small operators paying thousands a month for ad impressions while large chains sit on captive rewards balances customers don't even use. LYMX flips that asymmetry by giving every participating Business the network leverage that only chains used to have.

Kenny lives in Las Vegas and is recruiting LYMX's founding 25-Business cohort there.
