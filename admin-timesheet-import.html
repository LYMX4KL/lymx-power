Import timesheet from Excel

For paying hourly staff when clock-in data isn't available. Each row becomes a pre-approved timesheet line, ready for the payroll run. Re-importing the same person+date safely overwrites (never duplicates), and locked/paid days are never touched.

Step 1 · Get the template

Columns: email, work_date (YYYY-MM-DD), regular_hours, ot_hours, hourly_rate. Email must match how the staff member signs in. Leave hourly_rate blank to use their profile rate. OT is paid at 1.5×.
