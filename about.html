About LYMX

We're rebuilding how independent businesses grow together.

LYMX replaces fragmented loyalty programs and expensive ads with a shared-value network. One credit, every partner business — so the dollar a customer spends at one shop becomes a customer at the next.

Why we exist

Independent businesses spend ~12% of revenue on customer acquisition — most of it going to ad networks owned by the same handful of platforms. The cost goes up every year. The customers don't actually become loyal.

Meanwhile, every business runs its own loyalty program — points that only work in one place, never get redeemed, and gather digital dust. The customer doesn't feel rewarded. The business doesn't see returns.

LYMX flips this. Earn anywhere, redeem anywhere. The discount is the marketing budget. The customer carries real value across the network — and shows up at businesses they'd otherwise never have found.

It's not a loyalty program. It's a shared customer base that everyone in the network co-owns and contributes to.

We don't think the future of small business growth is more ads. It's businesses pooling their customer base and rewarding the customer for the loyalty they're already showing across the network.
— Kenny Lin, founder

What we believe

Transparent economics

Every fee, settlement, and commission is shown to every party. No hidden cuts. No surprise bills. The math is the product.

Pay-per-result

Businesses should only pay when something real happens — a customer, a transaction, a redemption. Not for impressions. Not for clicks.

The network owes the customer

1 LYMX = $0.01 across the entire network. Always. The customer's reward retains its value no matter where they spend it.
