How Vegas Wellness Group consolidated 3 loyalty programs across 6 locations and added 14% to monthly revenue.

Vegas Wellness Group operates 6 locations across the Las Vegas metro — three Pilates studios, two yoga studios, and one wellness spa. Joining LYMX Enterprise in November 2025 unified their fragmented loyalty stack and turned cross-location visits into a network growth engine.

Pilar Mendoza built Vegas Wellness Group over 11 years, starting with a single Pilates studio in 2014 and adding locations gradually. By 2025 she had three Pilates studios (Henderson, Summerlin, Spring Valley), two yoga studios (downtown + Boulder City) and one full-service wellness spa. Each had grown organically — and each had picked up its own loyalty system along the way.

Three loyalty systems, one customer base

The fragmented stack

  • Pilates studios: Mindbody Loyalty ($89/mo per location × 3 = $267)
  • Yoga studios: Glofox Loyalty ($65/mo × 2 = $130)
  • Spa: Mangomint Rewards ($120/mo)
  • Total monthly loyalty cost: $517
  • Customers had 3 different point balances they couldn't combine

The customer experience problem

  • A customer who took Pilates AND yoga had two separate loyalty accounts
  • Spa visits earned points unredeemable at any studio
  • Cross-location booking referrals were invisible — no attribution
  • ~22% of Pilar's customers used multiple locations, but the loyalty stack treated them as 6 different customers
"I had been talking to Mindbody for two years about adding cross-program point transfers. They had no plans to. The one thing all three of my systems had in common: each of them assumed they were the only loyalty program a customer needed. They weren't." Pilar Mendoza, CEO & Founder

Why LYMX Enterprise was the right fit

Pilar joined the LYMX Enterprise tier in November 2025 — before public launch. The Enterprise tier offers consolidated billing across multiple locations, a single LYMX program shared across all locations, custom reporting per location AND across the chain, and dedicated account management.

"I'd been waiting for someone to build a loyalty network that worked the way real customers behave — across multiple Businesses, with one balance. LYMX was the first one that didn't try to lock my customers into a single brand experience. It treated them like the multi-Business customers they actually are." Pilar Mendoza, CEO & Founder
