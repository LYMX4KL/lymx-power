📱 Lost or stolen phone

First priority is locking your wallet so no one can spend it. Then you recover on a new device.

  1. Lock your wallet immediately From any browser, go to wallet.lymx.com/lock, enter your phone number, and click "Lock wallet." We'll send a verification text to that number — if you can't get to that text yet, skip to step 2.
  2. Call our 24/7 emergency line: 1-833-LYMX-911 If your phone is gone and you can't get verification texts, call us. We verify identity through 3 security questions and can manually lock the wallet within 5 minutes.
  3. Get a new phone (or borrow one) Once you have any device with internet access, go to wallet.lymx.com and click "Recover wallet." If your number is the same, we send a verification text and you're back in within 60 seconds.
  4. If your number is also gone, we do manual ID verification Photo of government ID + a transaction history question (e.g., "Where did you spend LYMX last Tuesday?"). Usually resolved within 24 hours by our support team.
Did someone use your wallet between when you lost it and when you locked it? File a dispute via customer-dispute.html within 30 days. We refund unauthorized spending after investigation.

📞 I changed my phone number

Two paths depending on whether you still have access to the old number.

Path A — You still have the old number active

  1. Go to wallet → Settings → Phone number From your wallet on any device.
  2. Verify old number We text the old number — enter the code.
  3. Enter new number, verify it We text the new number — enter that code too.
  4. Done Wallet migrates instantly. All history, balance, referrals follow.

Path B — Old number is gone (deactivated, ported, etc.)

  1. Open a manual recovery ticket Email recover@lymx.com with your name + old number + new number + the date you switched.
  2. We verify identity Photo of government ID + a transaction question.
  3. Migration completes within 24-48 hours You'll get a text on the new number when it's done.

🔢 Forgot my PIN

Easiest scenario — your phone has the wallet, you just can't unlock it.

  1. Tap "Forgot PIN" on the lock screen Bottom of the PIN entry screen.
  2. Receive verification text We text the same number associated with the wallet.
  3. Enter the 6-digit code You're now in PIN reset mode.
  4. Set a new PIN Don't reuse your old one. Don't use 0000, 1234, or your birthday.
Failed PIN 5 times? The wallet auto-locks for 30 minutes. After 10 failed attempts, it locks until you call 1-833-LYMX-911. This is to prevent stranger-walks-by-with-your-phone scenarios.

📲 New phone, same number

Easiest of all. Your wallet isn't installed on the device — it's a web app at wallet.lymx.com.

  1. Open Safari/Chrome → wallet.lymx.com On the new phone.
  2. Enter phone number, get verification text Same number as before.
  3. Enter PIN If you remember it. If not — see "Forgot my PIN" above.
  4. Tap "Add to Home Screen" if you want one-tap access iOS: Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: ⋮ menu → Install app.

🚨 I think someone got into my wallet

Treat this as urgent. Lock the wallet first, investigate later.
  1. Lock the wallet wallet.lymx.com/lock or call 1-833-LYMX-911.
  2. Review recent activity Once locked, log in via customer-history.html — flag any transaction you didn't make.
  3. File a dispute for each unauthorized transaction Within 30 days. We refund verified unauthorized activity within 7 days of dispute approval.
  4. Reset PIN before unlocking Whoever got in may know your old PIN. Don't reuse it.
  5. Enable two-factor Settings → Security → Require text code on every login. Slightly more friction, much more secure.

⏳ Wallet shows "archived"

If your wallet has been inactive for 24+ months (no earn, no spend, no login), we archive it to keep our active-wallet list clean. Recovery is simple.

  1. Log in normally Same phone number, same PIN.
  2. Tap "Reactivate wallet" You'll see this banner instead of your normal balance.
  3. Verify your phone Standard text verification.
  4. Wallet restored All your old LYMX is intact — we never delete balances. Just paused.
Your LYMX never expires as long as the wallet exists. Even archived wallets keep their balance forever — we just hide them from active queries.

More questions

What if I lost my phone AND my number is gone?

Email recover@lymx.com with what you remember about the wallet — last Business you visited, approximate balance, when you joined. Combined with photo ID, our support team will recover your wallet within 24-48 hours.

Will I lose my LYMX if I don't recover quickly?

No. Your balance is held server-side and never expires (as long as the wallet exists). Take your time.

Do you charge for recovery?

No. All recovery — including manual ID verification — is free. Always.

Can I have a backup PIN or recovery question?

Settings → Security lets you add a backup recovery email and an optional security question. We recommend doing this when you set up the wallet — takes 30 seconds.

What if I'm in the middle of a purchase and locked out?

The Business has two options: (1) wait while you call recovery, or (2) charge you normally and apply LYMX retroactively after you recover. Most Businesses are happy to do option 2.
