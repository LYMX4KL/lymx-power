Honest TL;DR

If you're already deeply on Toast POS and the loyalty add-on is a checkbox on your existing setup, Toast Loyalty is fine. If you want repeat customers brought to you by other Businesses, the network effect is genuinely only available with LYMX. Three real scenarios — who wins each

Honest answers. Sometimes the answer is "it depends" — these are our best calls.

☕
Independent café · 1 location

"I run one café and want repeat customers"

You don't need a POS replacement. You need new customers and reasons for existing customers to come back. The LYMX network sends ~1,500 new local customers per month per Business via flywheel. Toast Loyalty sends zero — you have to bring them yourself.

Best fit: LYMX, by a clear margin.
🍕
Multi-location chain · 8 locations on Toast

"We're already deep in Toast across 8 locations"

You've spent 2 years building Toast workflows. Loyalty as a checkbox add-on is operationally simpler. LYMX is competitive long-term once we have a 100+ Business network in your cities (Phase 3, 2027).

Best fit today: Toast Loyalty. Revisit LYMX in Phase 3.
🍷
Independent restaurant + bar · 2-3 locations · Las Vegas

"We're building a multi-location restaurant brand in Vegas"

You're early enough to lock in Launch 25 perks. The LYMX Enterprise tier scales with you. Plus the Vegas-specific network is real now — your customers are already on LYMX from coffee & salon visits.

Best fit: LYMX Enterprise tier.

What each one is genuinely good at

LYMX

LYMX is the right answer when you...

  • Run an independent restaurant (1-3 locations) competing on community + loyalty
  • Want customers brought to you by the network, not just retained by your own program
  • Don't want a 3-year contract or POS replacement
  • Care about no expiration, low minimums, no app downloads for your customers
  • Want to refer other restaurants in your neighborhood and get paid for it
TS

Toast Loyalty is the right answer when you...

  • Are already on Toast POS and want one less vendor relationship
  • Operate 5+ locations with established Toast workflows
  • Need deep menu-engineering integration (modifier-level promotions, etc.)
  • Want kitchen display + ordering + loyalty in one stack from day 1
  • Aren't yet in a city with a meaningful LYMX Business network

For most independents in Las Vegas in 2026, LYMX is the better answer. For a regional chain on Toast, the answer flips. The only honest take is: it depends on your situation, and we'd rather you pick the right tool than the LYMX tool.
