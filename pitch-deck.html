LYMX 03 / 12

Local loyalty programs are designed to make customers give up

  • ~80% of points across loyalty programs are never redeemed.
  • The "breakage" is the program's profit margin — by design.
  • Local Businesses pay $200/month for a system optimized for someone else's outcome.
  • Customers carry 11 punch cards in their wallet and use almost none.

Sources: Bond Brand Loyalty 2025 · McKinsey 2024 · 47 Las Vegas Business owner interviews

LYMX 04 / 12

LYMX: shared loyalty for local Businesses

  • Earn at one Business, spend at any other in the network.
  • 1 LYMX = $0.01, always. No expiration. No fine print.
  • No app required — runs in the browser, add to home screen.
  • Each Business pays a flat $200/mo + a $850 sign-up. Customer-acquisition cost is the network.

The network flywheel
