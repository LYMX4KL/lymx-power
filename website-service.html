What if I miss the 3-month window?

Standard $500 still applies. The 3-month window is hard — we don't extend it past activation date.

What if I already have a website?

If it works fine on mobile and has your hours, photos, and menu, you don't need this. We can add a "Find us on LYMX" badge to your existing site for free — just send us your URL.

Can you customize beyond the template?

Up to a point. Your colors, photos, copy, sections, services — yes. Bespoke layouts, custom apps, e-commerce backends — that's a separate engagement (we'll quote separately). The $250 covers the templated build with your content.

Who actually builds the site?

A small in-house team plus 2 vetted local freelancers. You get the same project manager from kickoff through training. No call-center handoffs.
