Why this matters

📈

Each Business gets stronger when another joins

Unlike a stand-alone loyalty program, every new Business expands the value of existing Businesses' LYMX wallets. The opposite of competing for finite customer attention.

💵

CAC ≈ $0 on the network side

Customer-acquisition cost is paid by the Business that signs up the customer. Every other LYMX Business gets free access to that customer's spend.

🔁

Defensibility = network density

Once a city has 100+ LYMX Businesses, a competing network has to win 100+ Businesses to be useful. That's a real moat.
