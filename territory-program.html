★ Territory Program

Build a real local business as the LYMX operator for your city.

The Territory Program is for Partners who want to invest serious time and money to dominate a metro — open a local office, run road shows, train other Partners, and earn a percentage of every Business in the territory. Other Partners can still recruit anywhere; their commissions are never reduced. Your override is paid by LYMX, on top.

Three phases as the network grows

Start light, earn your way up. The Territory Leader role unlocks once the network proves it can sustain it.

Phase 1 · Now → 50 Businesses

Open recruiting

No formal territories yet
  • Anyone can recruit anywhere
  • First-mover lock via MGC tree (whoever recruits a Business has them as Direct forever)
  • Founding Partner badge for first 25 Partners
  • No territory bonuses yet
For: Everyone. Build your tree freely; the territory benefits unlock in Phase 2.
Phase 2 · 50 → 500 Businesses

City Champion

Recognition tier — earned automatically
  • Trigger: 5+ active Direct Businesses in one metro
  • Featured on homepage's "Powered by LYMX in [city]" callout
  • $200/mo co-op marketing budget for the metro
  • First right of refusal on inbound metro leads
  • Custom email like maya@sacramento.getlymx.com
  • Champion status is non-exclusive — others still recruit in your city
For: Active Partners who naturally cluster their recruits in one city. Light commitment, real recognition.
Phase 3 · 500+ Businesses

Territory Leader

Full city-operator role · application required
  • +3% override on every Business in your territory's monthly fee — even ones you didn't recruit. Paid by LYMX, not deducted from anyone.
  • 100% of inbound metro leads route to you for distribution
  • Public branding as Territory Leader
  • Right to host paid local events under the LYMX brand
  • Office staff can deliver Business onboarding (training delegation)
  • Eligible for stock option grant (Master Partner track)
For: Platinum Partners who'll commit to a real local presence — office space, events, training. Eight-figure income potential at scale.

Territory Leader — what it actually requires

The deal is real. So is the upside.

What you commit to

Real local presence + a quota or annual fee to maintain the territory.

  • 🏢
    Open a physical local office (or coworking space) Real address customers and other Partners can visit. Branded as the LYMX [City] office.
  • 🎤
    Host monthly road show / demo events Open to all Partners and prospect Businesses in your metro. You provide the venue and structure; LYMX provides the deck and demo materials.
  • 📚
    Run quarterly Partner training Bring new Partners through the Sales Kit, demo flow, and live practice. Your office staff can co-deliver.
  • 📊
    Hit volume quota or pay annual fee Either: 30+ Business activations in your metro per year, OR a $5,000 annual territory fee. Either way, you stay the Territory Leader.
  • 🤝
    Be the local face of LYMX Press, partnerships with local biz associations, civic events. You are LYMX in your city.

What you earn

Beyond your normal Partner commissions — which stay 100% intact.

  • 💰
    +3% override on every Business in territory $199 fee × 3% × every Business in your city = $5.97/mo per Business. At 100 Businesses, that's $597/mo passive.
  • 📍
    100% of inbound metro leads Anyone in your city contacting LYMX gets routed to you. You distribute to Partners in your tree or close them yourself.
  • ⭐
    Public Territory Leader branding Featured on getlymx.com homepage in your city's section. Public recognition, business credibility.
  • 🎟️
    Paid local events under LYMX brand You can charge admission for trade shows, demo days, training events. Keep the proceeds.
  • 📈
    Stock option grant track Top-performing Territory Leaders are eligible for the Master Partner stock option program. Real equity ownership in LYMX as the network scales.

Why this beats strict territory exclusivity

You earn the upside without locking out other Partners or capping their growth.

Other Partners are never blocked

Anyone, anywhere, can recruit any Business at any time. No "this city is taken" awkwardness. Your override is funded by LYMX out of platform margin, not deducted from anyone else's commission.

You only earn from real activity

The +3% is on Businesses' monthly fees — not on hot air. You're rewarded based on the size of the local economy you've helped build, not on which Partner happened to recruit which Business first.

You become a real local business owner

An office, a calendar of events, a roster of trained Partners, branded local marketing. You stop running a side hustle and start running a city operation. With a real career path forward.
