The basics 4 questions

What is LYMX?

LYMX is a shared loyalty currency. Earn it at any LYMX Business in your area. Spend it at any other LYMX Business. 1 LYMX always equals $0.01 of redemption value. Free to use, no app to download.

Is LYMX free for me?

Yes. Free forever. We never charge customers for the wallet, transactions, or anything else. Businesses pay to be on the network — that's how we fund operations.

Do I need to download an app?

No. The LYMX wallet runs in your browser at getlymx.com. Add it to your phone's home screen for one-tap access — looks and behaves like an app, but isn't one. Works on iPhone and Android.

How do I sign up?

Two ways. (1) Ask the cashier at any LYMX Business — they can sign you up at the register in 30 seconds. (2) Visit getlymx.com/signup directly. Either way, you'll need to verify your phone number. You get 100 LYMX as a sign-up bonus.

Earning LYMX 5 questions

How do I earn LYMX?

Show your LYMX QR to the cashier at any LYMX Business before paying. They scan it. LYMX is added to your wallet automatically. The default earn rate is 5 LYMX per $1 you spend (= 5% back).

Some Businesses give me more LYMX. Why?

That's a multiplier promo. Businesses can boost the LYMX rate (2× or 3× or 5×) on specific items, time periods, or events. It's their choice — and it's a way for them to drive specific behavior. Look for the multiplier badge on Business profiles.

What if I forget to show my QR?

You can ask the cashier to look up your account by phone number — it's the same flow. Most Businesses can also retroactively add LYMX to a transaction within 7 days if you show them a receipt.

Do I earn LYMX on tips, taxes, or delivery fees?

You earn on the pre-tip subtotal at restaurants. You don't earn on taxes or fees. You don't earn on delivery orders made through DoorDash or UberEats — those are separate from the LYMX network.

How do referrals work?

Share your unique referral code from your wallet. When a friend signs up using your code AND makes their first transaction, you both get 200 LYMX (= $2 each). No cap on how many friends you can refer. See your code in your referral page.

Spending LYMX 5 questions

How do I spend LYMX?

Tell the cashier "I'd like to apply LYMX to my bill" before they ring up your card. They'll prompt you to enter the amount. LYMX comes off the total, you pay the difference. Some Businesses also let you apply LYMX from your phone before checkout (Toast Pay, Square Online).

Does my LYMX expire?

Inactive LYMX (no earn or redemption activity for 24 months) may be archived but never permanently deleted. Activity resets the clock. We have never deleted anyone's LYMX.

Can I split a payment between LYMX and credit card?

Yes. Apply whatever LYMX amount you want, pay the rest with cash or card. There's no minimum or maximum redemption (other than your balance and the order total).

Can I redeem LYMX online?

Yes — at LYMX Businesses that accept online payments (Toast online, Square online). Enter your wallet phone number at checkout; the system applies the LYMX you choose. Most LYMX Businesses also accept LYMX in person.

Can I cash out my LYMX?

No. LYMX is a redemption credit, not a withdrawable balance. The value comes from spending it at LYMX Businesses (where it equals $0.01 each, always). It's not transferable to bank accounts or other currencies.

The network 3 questions

Where can I find LYMX Businesses?

Browse the directory at getlymx.com/browse. Filter by category, neighborhood, or current promo. Or look for the "We accept LYMX" window cling on storefronts.

Why aren't there more Businesses where I live?

We're expanding city by city. Las Vegas is live now (Q3 2026). Phoenix and Reno are next (Q4 2026). Vote for your city at /vote — most-voted cities go first.

Can I suggest a Business to join LYMX?

Yes. Tell them about LYMX, or send us their info at hello@getlymx.com. We'll reach out (with mention of you, if you want). If they end up signing up, you get 500 bonus LYMX as a thank-you.

My account 4 questions

How do I see my balance?

Open your wallet at getlymx.com/wallet. Balance shows at the top, with cash equivalent and your transaction history below.

I changed my phone number. How do I update it?

Go to Settings → Phone. We'll send a verification code to your new number to confirm. Your wallet and balance follow you to the new number — nothing is lost.

Can I have multiple wallets (work + personal)?

Yes — one wallet per phone number. Most customers find one wallet is plenty since LYMX works across the entire network already.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete account. We'll first offer to export your data, then permanently delete your wallet, balance, and transaction history within 30 days. This is irreversible.

Security & privacy 3 questions

Can someone steal my LYMX if my phone is lost?

Your LYMX QR is dynamic — it refreshes every 10 seconds, so a screenshot can't be replayed. If your phone is lost, sign in to your wallet from another device and disable the lost device under Settings → Security. We can also remotely freeze a wallet if you contact support.

What data does LYMX have on me?

Your phone number (required), name (optional), email (optional), and your transaction history. We never see your credit card numbers — those go through Square / Stripe. We never sell data. See privacy policy for the complete picture.

Do Businesses see my personal info?

Businesses see your name (if you provided one) at the time of transaction. They don't see your full transaction history, balance, or any other personal data. Aggregate stats only.
