How LYMX works for you

No complicated points tables. One credit, fixed value, usable at any participating business.

1

Earn LYMX

Shop, eat, pay rent, charge your EV — any qualifying activity at a LYMX partner rewards you with LYMX.

2

Redeem your LYMX at any participating Business. 1 LYMX = $0.01 everywhere on the network — fixed redemption value, no fine print.
