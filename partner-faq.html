Applying to be a Partner 4 questions

How do I become a Partner?

Apply at /partner-signup. The application takes 5 minutes — short bio, why you want to be a Partner, your geographic area, ID verification. We review applications weekly and notify you within 7 days.

Do I have to pay anything to become a Partner?

No. There's no application fee, no buy-in, no minimum purchase, no recurring Partner fee. The Partner program is a referral program — you earn commission on Businesses you sign. You don't pay to participate.

What's the approval rate?

About 35% of applicants are approved. We're selective because Partners represent the LYMX network when they prospect. We approve people who have either (a) experience selling to small Businesses, (b) deep relationships in their local Business community, or (c) both. We reject applications that look mass-applied or have unclear motivations.

If I'm rejected, can I reapply?

Yes — after 90 days. Use the time to either build local relationships or run a small project that demonstrates ability to engage with Businesses. Many of our best Partners were rejected on first attempt.

Commission & earnings 5 questions

How much do Partners earn?

Commission is 9% of monthly LYMX spend on Businesses you personally signed (Production), plus override percentages on downline (3% G1, 2% G2, 1% G3). Plus a one-time $500 bonus for each Business you sign. See the commission calculator for live modeling. Most Active Partners earn $1,500–4,000/mo; Master Partners earn $8,000+/mo.

How is commission calculated?

Each month, we calculate each Business's monthly subscription + LYMX activity fees (3% processing + per-order fees beyond 3,000) and apply your tier's percentage. Example: a single-location Business at $199/mo subscription with $50 of monthly LYMX fees = ~$249 total. Direct Partner gets 9% of that = ~$22/mo. G1 Partner gets 3% = ~$7/mo. Read commission engine docs for the math.

Why does commission decline by generation?

Direct work earns more than passive override. The 9% direct → 3%/2%/1% structure rewards Partners for their own production while still creating incentive to recruit and mentor downline. This is FTC-compliant — direct sales are always the largest source of income for active Partners.

What about LYMX commissions vs. cash?

You earn BOTH. Cash commission is what we discussed above. Separately, you also earn matching LYMX commissions credited to your customer LYMX wallet — equal to your cash commission percentage but in LYMX. So if you earn $27 cash on a Business, you also earn 2,700 LYMX in your wallet. The LYMX is spendable at any LYMX Business.

Are there bonuses?

Three: (1) $500 one-time per Business signed, (2) milestone bonuses at 5/10/25 own Businesses ($500/$1,500/$5,000), (3) Master Partner status when you hit thresholds — comes with bumped rates and additional perks. See master playbook.

Downline & recruitment 4 questions

How do generations work exactly?

Generations only apply to Partners, not Businesses. Your own Businesses = "Production" (9% commission, never a generation). Partners you sign = G1 (you earn 3% on their Production). Partners they sign = G2 (you earn 2%). Partners G2 signs = G3 (you earn 1%). The chain stops at G3 — there is no G4.

Why does my Business in my tree count as Production, not G1?

Because Businesses aren't Partners. Generations track Partner-to-Partner relationships only. A Business is the end customer; it can't recruit other Businesses. So if you have 5 own Businesses, that's 5 in Production — not 5 in G1.

Can I recruit anyone, or are there geographic restrictions?

No geographic restrictions. You can recruit Partners and Businesses anywhere in the LYMX network. In practice, geographic concentration helps — referrals work best when you and your downline are working the same market.

What if my G1 Partner becomes inactive?

If your G1 Partner stops working (no new Business signed in 6 months), they remain in your tree but their Production stops growing — meaning your G1 override doesn't grow either. They can resume anytime. We don't auto-remove inactive Partners from your tree — you keep your override on whatever they do.

Payouts 3 questions

When do I get paid?

Cash commissions are paid via ACH on the 5th of each month for the prior month's earnings. LYMX commissions are credited the same day to your network LYMX balance. See payouts page.

Are there minimum payout thresholds?

For cash: $25 minimum. If you earn less than $25 in a month, it rolls into the next month until you hit threshold. For LYMX: no minimum — we credit your wallet immediately.

Can I get paid faster than monthly?

For now, monthly only — it aligns with our settlement cycle. We're considering bi-weekly payouts in 2027 as a Master Partner perk.

Compliance & rules 4 questions

What can I claim about Partner income in my pitches?

You can reference the income calculator (which models earnings), or share your own earnings honestly. You CANNOT claim guaranteed earnings, "make $X/mo" without disclaimers, or imply that LYMX is an investment. Read the compliance brief for specifics. FTC violations can result in Partner suspension.

What can I NOT do as a Partner?

(1) Cold-email purchased lists — that's CAN-SPAM violation. (2) Make earnings claims without FTC-required disclaimers. (3) Misrepresent LYMX features in pitches. (4) Use the LYMX brand on materials we haven't approved (use the approved templates). (5) Pressure prospects with high-stakes urgency tactics. See Partner Agreement for the full list.

Do I need to register as a sales rep in my state?

Most states don't require this for referral-based commission programs like LYMX. Your business structure is up to you — most Partners file as sole proprietors and report income on Schedule C. See Partner tax guide.

What happens if I violate the agreement?

First violation: warning + education. Second: 30-day suspension of commissions and recruitment. Third: removal from Partner program. Decisions are reviewed by senior staff — see our enforcement process.

If I want to leave 2 questions

Can I quit being a Partner anytime?

Yes, anytime. No notice required. Email partners@getlymx.com or click "Deactivate" in your dashboard. Outstanding commissions through your last full month will be paid out as scheduled. Future commissions you would have earned go to LYMX (we don't pass them to your upline).

What happens to my downline if I leave?

They remain Partners and keep their own commission structure. Your G1 Partners' G1 → become someone else's G1 (depends on case); their downline stays intact. Most Partners' departure has minimal impact on their downline because the downline's direct earnings come from their own work.

What happens to my commission if a Business I signed gets sold?

If the new owner stays in the LYMX network (the standard asset-transfer flow), your Partner attribution stays with the account — you keep earning commission on it. If the new owner declines LYMX and the account winds down, your commission ends when the account exits the network (after the standard 90-day wind-down period).

You don't pay to join, and you don't pay to maintain Partner status. By becoming a Partner you agree to the Partner Agreement.
