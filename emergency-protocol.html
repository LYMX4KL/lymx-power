🚨 My phone was stolen / lost — wallet might be compromised

Urgent · do this NOW

If your phone is gone and you're worried about your LYMX wallet:

  1. Sign into your LYMX wallet from any other device — go to getlymx.com/wallet. Authentication is by your phone number, but you'll get an SMS code at your number once you have a new SIM.
  2. Click "Lost device" in Security settings — this immediately revokes the QR access on the lost device. Anyone holding it can no longer scan to spend your LYMX.
  3. If you can't sign in, email emergency@getlymx.com with: your phone number, your name, last 4 of any recent receipt. We freeze the wallet within 30 minutes.
  4. Once you have a new device + SIM, sign back in. Your balance is preserved — we never delete LYMX, even from frozen wallets.
Speed matters: Stolen wallet recovery is free if reported within 30 days. After that, recovery depends on case specifics.

💸 LYMX I should have earned isn't showing in my wallet

High · within 24 hr

You bought something at a LYMX Business but the LYMX didn't appear in your wallet, or the amount is wrong:

  1. Check the receipt. If LYMX is shown on the receipt, it's recorded — may take up to 60 sec to appear in the wallet. Refresh.
  2. If not on the receipt, ask the cashier to retroactively add LYMX (most can within 7 days). They scan your QR and the system catches up.
  3. If the cashier can't help, file a customer dispute. We investigate within 1 business day. If we made an error, we credit immediately.
  4. Promo multiplier didn't apply? Same dispute flow — include the date, business name, and what multiplier you think should have applied.

💳 I think I got double-charged or my redemption was wrong

High · within 24 hr

Card was charged twice, or LYMX was deducted twice for the same order, or the redemption amount looks wrong:

  1. If it's a card double-charge: contact your card issuer first (Visa, Mastercard, etc.). LYMX doesn't process card payments — Square/Stripe/Toast does. The Business or your card issuer fixes this.
  2. If it's a LYMX double-deduction, file a dispute. We can confirm in our logs whether the deduction happened twice and refund the duplicate.
  3. If the amount is wrong, check the receipt against your wallet history. Sometimes a multi-step redemption appears as multiple lines but is actually one transaction.

🏪 A Business won't accept my LYMX even though they're listed

Medium · within 7 days

You went to a LYMX Business and they said they don't accept LYMX, or the cashier seemed confused:

  1. Check their listing at getlymx.com/browse. If their listing has a "Leaving the LYMX network" banner, they're in their 90-day wind-down — but should still accept.
  2. Be patient and helpful with the cashier. Say "I have LYMX — please scan my QR before you ring up." Many cashiers are new.
  3. If they still won't take it, file a dispute with the Business name, date, and what happened. We follow up directly with the Business.
  4. You should never lose LYMX on a refused redemption. If the Business eventually accepts but you've already paid full price, we adjust and credit you the LYMX afterward.

⚠️ I see a transaction in my wallet I didn't make

Urgent · do this NOW

Someone else is using your wallet — possibly fraud:

  1. Freeze your wallet immediately via Settings → Security → Freeze wallet. Stops all activity in 5 sec.
  2. Email emergency@getlymx.com with: the unrecognized transaction (date, amount, business), and any other details.
  3. Change your authentication. If you have a new SIM/device since the issue started, that's done. If not, complete the verification flow we'll send you.
  4. Recovery: we investigate within 4 hours. If we confirm fraud, the LYMX is restored. Card-based fraud is between you and your card issuer.

🌐 LYMX system is down — I can't open my wallet

Medium · we usually fix in < 30 min

The LYMX wallet won't load, or you're getting error messages:

  1. Check status.getlymx.com first — if there's an active incident, we're already working on it. ETA usually posted.
  2. If status shows operational but you can't connect: clear your browser cache, refresh, try a different network (wifi vs. cellular).
  3. If the issue persists, email support@getlymx.com with: device type, browser, what error you're seeing.
  4. During an outage, your balance is safe. Just deferred — once we recover, all transactions queue up and replay correctly.

How to prevent emergencies

A few habits that make every emergency easier to recover from.

📱
Set up a backup access methodIn Settings, add a backup email and a recovery phone (a friend or family member's phone). Lets us reach you even if your primary is gone.
🔒
Enable security notificationsGet an email any time someone signs in to your wallet, or a redemption over $50 happens. Catches issues fast.
📸
Save your receipts (digital is fine)If you ever need to retroactively add LYMX, having the receipt saves arguing with the cashier. Most LYMX Businesses email digital receipts.
🆔
Verify your phone number is currentIf you change your phone number, update it in Settings before you stop using the old one. Otherwise account recovery gets more complicated.
