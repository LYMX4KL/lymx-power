v0.3Buy/sell mechanic — final structure

Apr 27, 2026

🛡Security & legal positioning

  • Removed all "escrow" framing from public copy. LYMX does not hold or transmit money. The buy/sell mechanic is structured as ordinary accounts payable / receivable.
  • Issuance: Business purchases units from LYMX at $0.008 (80% of face)
  • Redemption: LYMX buys units back from the redeeming Business at the same $0.008
  • 20% gap is the redeeming Business's customer-acquisition discount — not retained by LYMX

↻Changed

  • Issuance rate is now locked by LYMX at activation — Business cannot change mid-contract. Promotional multipliers (time-bound, item-specific) layer on top via the new Promotions page.
  • POS, Settlement, and dashboards updated to reflect the new "unit purchase / unit buy-back" terminology end-to-end
