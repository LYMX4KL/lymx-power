Oakline Kitchen

★ 4.8 (214 reviews) · Asian fusion · Bar · $$ · Downtown Las Vegas Live on LYMX

About

Oakline is a chef-driven Asian fusion kitchen and bar in downtown Las Vegas. Chef Lin trained in Taipei and Brooklyn, and the menu reflects both — clean seasonal plates, hand-pulled noodles, and a small but obsessive cocktail program. Open since 2022, and a regular spot for date night, late dinner, or happy hour with the team.

Earn 5 LYMX per $1 spent on food, drinks, and to-go. Redeem any LYMX balance toward your bill — there's no minimum and no exclusions on dishes or drinks.
