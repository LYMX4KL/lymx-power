LYMX Power Staff Working Agreement (v1)

As a member of the LYMX team, you agree to:
• Work the hours scheduled and accepted via the LYMX schedule system.
• Clock in at the start of each shift and clock out at the end. Outside scheduled hours, work features are turned off.
• Keep customer + partner information confidential. Don't share login credentials.
• Submit time-off requests in advance through your dashboard.
• Use the chat / conversations tools for all work communication so it stays on-record.

This agreement can be updated. You'll be re-prompted to re-accept any material changes.
