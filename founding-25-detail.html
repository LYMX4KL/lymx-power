About Sage & Stone

Riya Patel and her husband Aman opened Sage & Stone in 2024 after 12 years of weekend home-baking from a 200 sq ft kitchen. The bakery is named for the two ingredients in their first sourdough starter — a cutting from a friend's sage plant and the river stone they kept by the dough.

Naturally-leavened sourdough, scratch-made pastries, and a 4-bean espresso bar. They joined LYMX in week 8 as Launch 25 Business #6.
