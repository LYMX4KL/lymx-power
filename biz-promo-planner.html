Plan your LYMX promotions like a pro

A 12-month framework for using LYMX promos strategically — driving traffic on slow days, capturing value on peak days, and building customer habits over time.

The 5 LYMX promo principles

  1. Promote into slow time, capture peak time. 2x LYMX on Tuesday lunch → drives traffic. Don't 2x LYMX during your busy Friday — you're paying for traffic you'd already have.
  2. Always set a daily cap. "First 50 customers" or "$2,000 LYMX max." Open-ended promos are how Businesses lose 5-figures in a weekend.
  3. Target a behavior, not a discount. "Visit 3 days in a row → 500 LYMX bonus" creates habits. "10% off" is just a margin hit.
  4. Stack promos with your slow inventory. 2x LYMX on the menu items you're trying to push. Use customer earn to subsidize your overstock.
  5. Measure, not vibes. Every promo needs a baseline (last 4 weeks same day) and a result (revenue + new customers). Run promo tournaments — keep what wins.

The 8 promo types that work

Multiplier promoMost common

2x or 3x LYMX during a specific window. Drives traffic into a specific time slot.

When: Slow time slots (Tue lunch, Sun breakfast)
5/$1 base × 2 = 10 LYMX/$1 → cost: $0.05 extra per $1

First-time visit bonusAcquisition

One-time 250-500 LYMX bonus when a customer visits your Business for the first time.

When: Always on (network growth phase)
500 LYMX = $5 marketing cost per new customer

Visit streakHabit-building

Bonus LYMX when customer visits N days in a row or N times in a week.

When: Café, juice bar, recurring purchases
3 visits → +500 LYMX. Costs $5, locks in ~$60 LTV.

Threshold promoAOV lift

Extra LYMX on purchases above $X. Lifts average ticket size.

When: Restaurant, retail, services
Spend $30+ → 2x LYMX. Drives upsell to $30 minimum.

Group / referralWord-of-mouth

Bonus LYMX when customer brings a friend who hasn't been before.

When: Anytime, especially launch phase
+200 LYMX each. $4 marketing per new customer.

Birthday bonusRetention

2x LYMX during customer's birthday week.

When: Always on, customers love it
Auto-applied if customer set birthday in profile

Anniversary promoMarketing event

Triple LYMX on your Business' LYMX-anniversary day. Marketing hook.

When: Once a year
Cap at first 100 customers. Guaranteed press hit.

Inventory clearanceMargin saver

Extra LYMX on specific items you need to move (overstocked, near-expiration).

When: Specific inventory pressure
3x LYMX on items A/B/C. Burn through stock without permanent discount.

Set up a promo in 90 seconds

  1. Dashboard → Promos → New promo
  2. Pick promo type from the 8 above
  3. Set start time, end time, daily cap (always set a cap)
  4. Choose customer eligibility (all / new / repeat / specific tags)
  5. Optional: link to specific menu items
  6. Schedule and confirm. Live test on staff phones.
