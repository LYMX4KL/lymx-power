# Cold Outreach Email — "We Pay You to Accept Our Customers"

> **Use case:** outbound email to Founding-25 business candidates.
> **Vibe:** plain, direct, no marketing fluff. The pitch is so unusual it doesn't need dressing up.
> **Voice:** Kenny, first person.
> **Drafted:** 2026-05-21

---

## Subject line (A/B test these)

A. **I'd like to pay you to accept our customers.**
B. **Free customer traffic — and we pay you when they buy.**
C. **A loyalty deal where the loyalty platform eats the cost, not you.**
D. **Quick question about [Business Name]:** *(opens curiosity gap)*

> Lead with **(A)** for cold first-touch. Use **(D)** if you're following up after a no-reply.

---

## Body (~140 words — under 30-second read)

Hi [First name],

Quick one. I'm Kenny — I run LYMX, a loyalty network for independent businesses around [city/area].

Most loyalty platforms ask *you* to absorb the discount when customers redeem points. LYMX does the opposite: **when a customer redeems their LYMX rewards at your store, we pay you cash for the redemption.** You don't eat the discount. We do.

Right now there are **[LIVE_NUM] customers** in our network sitting on **~$[LIVE_USD] in rewards**, ready to spend at the next business that opens nearby. We're hand-picking the first **25 Founding Businesses** to seed the network. **3 months free, then $199/month**, with a permanent feature spot on getlymx.com/browse.

15-minute call this week to see if [Business Name] fits?

— Kenny
[phone] · kenny@lymxpower.com · [https://getlymx.com/business](https://getlymx.com/business)

---

## Variables to swap before sending

| Placeholder | Where to find the value |
|---|---|
| `[First name]` | Owner's first name |
| `[Business Name]` | Their legal/storefront name |
| `[city/area]` | Their actual neighborhood (Reno, Vegas, etc.) |
| `[LIVE_NUM]` | Refresh from getlymx.com/business hero before sending — read the "customers earning LYMX" number |
| `[LIVE_USD]` | Refresh from getlymx.com/business hero — read the "≈ USD value" number |
| `[phone]` | Kenny's direct line |

---

## Follow-up sequence (if no reply)

- **Day 3:** Short bump — *"Hi [First name], wanted to make sure my note didn't get buried. Still happy to do 15 minutes if you're curious."*
- **Day 7:** Value-add — share one specific data point ("Since I emailed, 4 more customers joined LYMX in your zip code — they're carrying ~$X in rewards.")
- **Day 14:** Soft close — *"Last touch — I'll stop pinging. If LYMX ever makes sense for [Business Name], my line is always open."*
