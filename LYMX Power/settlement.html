QuickBooks import (3 steps)
  1. Open QuickBooks Online → + New → Bank Deposit or Bill
  2. Select Import data → Bank transactions → pick the downloaded lymx-settlement.csv
  3. Map columns: Date → Date, Memo → Description, Amount → Amount. Categorize unit purchases to "Marketing & advertising" and buy-backs to "Other income".
