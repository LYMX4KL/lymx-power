Earning & redeeming LYMX
How do I earn LYMX?
Make any qualifying purchase or activity at a LYMX Business. The platform sets each Business's earn rate at activation (typically 2–10 LYMX per $1 spent). LYMX shows up in your account within minutes of the transaction clearing.
What is 1 LYMX worth?
1 LYMX = $0.01 across the entire network. The redemption value is fixed — no tier conversions, no fine print. 1,000 LYMX = $10 of redemption value at any participating Business.
How do I redeem at checkout?
In the LYMX app, tap "Scan to redeem" and point at the QR code at the partner's register. The app shows your balance and lets you choose how much to apply to your bill. The partner's POS handles the rest automatically.
Do my LYMX expire?
No. Earned LYMX never expire as long as your account is active.
Reviews & LYMX
How do I earn LYMX for writing a review?
After a verified visit, you'll see "Pending review" prompts in your dashboard. Write a review (min 30 characters) and earn 50 LYMX. Add a photo to earn 50 more. Reviews must be from a verified visit — we cross-reference your transaction history.
What's the referral program?
Each member gets a unique referral code. When a friend joins LYMX through your code and makes a first purchase, you earn 500 LYMX. Track your referrals in your dashboard.
Account & privacy
How do I update my account details?
In the LYMX app or on the website, go to your dashboard and tap your profile in the top-right. You can update email, phone, photo, and notification preferences from there.
What data does LYMX collect?
Just enough to make the network work: which partners you transact at, your LYMX balance and activity, and your reviews. We don't sell data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for the full list.
Plans & billing
What does LYMX actually cost me each month?
$199/month subscription + a settlement number that's typically near zero. If you issue 14,200 LYMX and customers redeem 8,400 LYMX at your business, the net settlement is roughly $36. Your real "marketing cost" only shows up when LYMX is actually redeemed — meaning a real customer actually transacted.
Can I switch plans?
Yes, anytime, from your dashboard. Plan changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Moving from Pro to Enterprise activates your public listing on getlymx.com within 24 hours.
What happens if I cancel?
No contracts. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Outstanding LYMX you've issued stays redeemable for 90 days; we settle the final bill at the end of that window.
Operations & integrations
What POS systems do you integrate with?
Square, Shopify, Toast, Clover, and Stripe at launch. We also support a receipt-scan fallback for businesses that don't want to integrate at the POS level. Setup is part of the 5-step onboarding.
What if I issue more LYMX than I take in?
That means you've successfully sent customers into the network with money to spend, and other partners welcomed them. Your settlement bill on the 1st covers the difference. Over time most partners settle close to net-zero; high-traffic businesses typically end up net positive.
Can I run my own promotions?
Yes. From your dashboard you can offer 2× LYMX windows, redemption perks, first-visit bonuses, and category-targeted promotions. Most are live within 5 minutes of posting.
MGC commissions
How does the MGC program work?
Refer Enterprise businesses through your unique code. Earn $100 cash bonus + 9% ongoing on each direct (G1) referral's monthly fee + 3%/2%/1% override on G2/G3 referrals. Mirror LYMX commissions on transaction volume across all 3 generations. See full rules at business.html#earn.
When do I get paid?
Cash commissions are paid via ACH on the 5th of each month. LYMX commissions credit to your LYMX balance immediately. We issue 1099-NEC forms each January for the prior tax year.
Can I become a partner without owning a business?
Yes — independent Partners are welcome. Apply through the partner program page; there's no fee and no minimum. The program rewards you for activated Enterprise businesses, not your own LYMX activity.
