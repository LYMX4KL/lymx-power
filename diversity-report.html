Our 2026 commitments

What we said we'd do, what we did, and what we're going to focus on next.

✓ Launch 25 minority-owned majority

61% of our Launch 25 Las Vegas Businesses are minority-owned. We picked them; this wasn't accidental.

✓ Partner program: no degree gate

Application asks about local relationships, not credentials. Partners come from many backgrounds.

Where we fall short — and what we'll do

  • Engineering team is not diverse enough. 1 of 3 engineers is a woman. 0 are Black or Hispanic. We have an active engineering search; we're committing to interview at least 5 candidates from underrepresented backgrounds before any hire.
  • Operations leadership is one person. We can't claim diversity at a leadership level when there's only one person at it. Once we expand ops leadership in 2027, we'll be deliberate about who we recruit.
  • The founder is the only "leader" right now. Until we have a second leadership hire, we lack diversity at the top simply because the team is one person tall above the IC layer. Hiring is the answer; it's also imperfect.
  • We don't yet measure pay equity. Team is too small to do meaningful pay-band statistical analysis. We've committed to publish Mean Gender Pay Gap data once headcount > 25.

How to apply, partner, or push back

If you want to work at LYMX HQ, see careers. If you're a Business owner from an under-represented background applying to the Launch 25 in your city — let us know during application; we'll prioritize the review. If you're a Partner who wants to mentor others from your community, we'll match you. And if you read this report and think we got something wrong — email Kenny directly at kenny@getlymx.com. He reads everything.
