Your monthly LYMX Partner cadence

After the first month, your work falls into a rhythm. This is the standing monthly checklist top Partners run. Block it in your calendar — not optional. The Partners who run this rhythm consistently are the ones in the top 10 leaderboard.

📊Pipeline

Pre-week pipeline review, prospecting, follow-ups

~12 hrs/month

🤝Customer (Business)

Existing Business check-ins, retention, upsell

~6 hrs/month

🎓Learning

Bootcamp, mentor sessions, self-study

~4 hrs/month

📋Admin

CRM hygiene, expense reports, commission audit

~2 hrs/month

Week 1 of the month

Pipeline kickoff
  • Pipeline review with mentorRun through every active prospect with mentor. They'll spot leaks (e.g., "you forgot to follow up with X").
  • Build new prospect list (target 25 fresh names)Walk a new neighborhood. Add to CRM. Filter to qualified.
  • Schedule 6-8 first conversations for week 2Reach out to top 10 from new list. Aim for 60%+ to schedule.
  • Re-engage "maybe" pile from prior monthAnyone you tagged "recheck in 30 days" — now's the time.
  • Review the month's expected close revenueFor commission planning. Be honest with yourself.

Week 2

Discovery week
  • Run 8-12 first conversationsFrom the prospects you scheduled in week 1.
  • Schedule 3-5 formal pitch meetings for week 3Convert qualified discoveries into full meetings.
  • Update CRM after each conversationLog within 24 hours or you'll forget specifics.
  • Send week-2 follow-up emailsTemplates from partner-followup-templates.
  • Mid-month mentor check-in30-min call. What's stuck? What's working?

Week 3

Close week
  • Run 3-5 formal pitch meetingsUse partner-pitch-template. Custom revenue-calc for each.
  • Sign 1-3 new BusinessesMedian monthly target. Top performers do 4-5.
  • Hand off signed Businesses to onboardingSend their info to onboarding team. Track activation in CRM.
  • Touch 3-5 existing BusinessesCall/visit Businesses you signed in prior months. How are they doing? Any concerns?
  • Send "thinking of you" notesTo "yes-but-not-now" prospects. Keep warmth without pestering.

Week 4

Wrap + plan
  • Saturday bootcamp attendanceBoth bootcamp Saturdays. Don't skip — top Partner share their wins here.
  • Customer (Business) check-ins on tier-1 accountsThe 3-5 Businesses doing best. Are they happy? What more can LYMX do for them?
  • Commission auditVerify last month's commission landed right. commission-calc for math check.
  • Self-review: what worked, what didn't30 minutes alone with notebook. What pattern needs to change next month?
  • Plan next month's territory + themeSometimes a focus theme helps — "this month I'm focusing on coffee shops" or "this month I'm calling old maybes."
  • Update CRM hygieneArchive cold leads. De-dup. Re-tag. 30 minutes.

Always-on tasks

  • Daily: Update CRM after every Business interaction. 5 minutes/day.
  • Daily: Check inbox for any inbound from prospects. Respond within 4 hours during business hours.
  • Weekly: 30-min mentor session. Don't skip even when busy.
  • Weekly: Review pipeline pivot table. Where are deals stuck?
  • Bi-weekly: Read latest LYMX product updates. Changes to commission, integrations, features.

Quarterly add-ons

Every 3rd month, add these to your week 4:

  • Comprehensive territory audit — where's saturated, where's untapped
  • Revenue projection for next quarter (your CRM math + pipeline conversion rates)
  • Tax check-in (especially Q4) — see partner-tax-guide
  • Mentor relationship review — still working? Want a different mentor?
  • Public leaderboard goal-setting — where do you want to land next quarter?

When the rhythm slips

Most Partners who quit in months 4-6 do so because the rhythm slipped — they had one bad week, then two, then they were behind on CRM, then they couldn't see their pipeline, then they got demoralized. The fix:

  1. Tell your mentor immediately
  2. Block 4 hours, do CRM hygiene + plan reset
  3. Email partner support for a recovery call if you need it
  4. Don't try to "catch up" — restart the rhythm fresh next Monday
