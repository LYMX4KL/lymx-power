Plain-English glossary

Every word you'll see in your wallet, on receipts, or in our promos — defined in regular human language. (For Business and Partner glossary, see the full glossary.)

ABCDEFILMNPQRSTW
A
Active walletvs. archived
A wallet that has had at least one transaction in the past 24 months.
If you don't earn or spend for 24+ months, your wallet is automatically archived. Recoverable anytime — your balance is never deleted.
Available LYMXvs. pending
The portion of your balance you can actually spend right now.
Recently earned LYMX may show as "pending" for up to 24 hours before settling to "available." Both count toward your visible balance.
B
Balance
Your total LYMX, both available and pending. 100 LYMX = $1.
Businesscapital B
A merchant on the LYMX network. We capitalize "Business" throughout the wallet to distinguish from generic businesses.
"218 Businesses are on the network in Vegas as of August 2026."
C
Cross-redemption
Spending LYMX you earned at one Business to pay at a different Business.
You earned 1,000 LYMX at the coffee shop, you spend it at the salon. The salon gets settled by the coffee shop's account through LYMX clearing.
D
Dispute
A formal claim that a charge or earn was incorrect. Must be filed within 30 days. Resolved within 7 business days.
"I see a charge I didn't make" → file dispute → we investigate → refund if verified.
E
Earn rate
How many LYMX you get per dollar spent at a specific Business. Set by each Business at activation; commonly 5/$1 or 3/$1.
A 5/$1 Business: spend $20, earn 100 LYMX. A 3/$1 Business: spend $20, earn 60 LYMX.
Expiration
LYMX has no expiration date as long as your wallet stays active (one transaction per 24 months). We don't expire balances.
F
First-visit bonus
A one-time LYMX bonus some Businesses offer when you visit them for the first time on the network. Usually 250-500 LYMX.
I
Issuance
When a Business creates new LYMX in your wallet (i.e., you earn). Issuance is settled — the Business pays the network for the value of LYMX they create.
L
Leaving the LYMX network
A Business that has filed a 90-day cancellation but is still accepting LYMX during the wind-down period.
Their listing shows "Leaving the LYMX network — still accepting LYMX." During this 90 days, they continue to honor LYMX but stop issuing new LYMX.
LYMX/ˈlɪməks/
The shared loyalty rewards used across the LYMX network. L-Y-M-X. Pronounced "lim-ex."
"Loyalty Multi-eXchange" — the original full name. Just call it LYMX. Not money, not currency, not crypto — just rewards you earn at one Business and redeem at another.
M
Multiplier
A temporary boost on your earn rate. Common ones: "2x LYMX" (double earn), "3x weekend special."
If a Business is normally 5/$1 and runs a 2x weekend, you earn 10 LYMX per dollar Friday-Sunday.
N
Network
All LYMX Businesses, taken together. The promise of LYMX is that what you earn anywhere works everywhere — that's the network effect.
P
Pending LYMX
LYMX that has been issued but hasn't fully settled yet. Visible in your balance, not yet spendable. Usually clears in 24 hours.
PIN
The 4-digit code that unlocks your wallet. Set during sign-up. Can be reset anytime if forgotten (see recovery).
Promo
A time-limited offer from a Business — usually a multiplier (2x LYMX) or a bonus (first 50 customers earn extra 200 LYMX).
Q
QR code
The black-and-white square image in your wallet that the cashier scans to identify your wallet. Refreshes every 60 seconds for security. Can also be replaced by giving the cashier your phone number.
R
Redeem / redemption
Spending your LYMX at a Business. 100 LYMX = $1. Show your QR, cashier scans, your balance drops by the amount applied.
Referral
Two kinds: customer referral (you invite a friend → 100 LYMX bonus when they make first purchase) and Business referral (you suggest a Business join → 500 LYMX bonus when they activate).
S
Settlement
The behind-the-scenes accounting that moves money between Businesses when you cross-redeem. The Business that issued your LYMX pays the Business where you spent it. Customers don't see this — it just works.
Sign-up bonus
First-time customer bonus. Some Businesses give 200 LYMX for opening your wallet at their location. Network-wide promo, varies by month.
T
Transaction
Any LYMX activity — earn, spend, transfer, refund. All transactions visible in History.
Transfer
Sending LYMX from your wallet to another wallet (typically a friend or family member's). Free, instant if recipient has an active wallet.
W
Wallet
Your account on LYMX. The web app at wallet.lymx.com. Tied to your phone number. Holds your balance, history, settings.
Wind-down
90-day period after a Business announces they're leaving the network. They stop issuing LYMX immediately but continue honoring redemption for 90 days. See refund policy for full details.
