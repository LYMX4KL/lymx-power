Why a qualifying program?

The Founding 25 Partners are the network's elder generation. You proved the model works. New Partners look up to you. New Businesses trust you because you've been around.

The grandfather perks are recognition for that. But they only stay valuable if Founding 25 Partners continue showing up — training the next wave, mentoring new Partners, attending cooperative events that strengthen the network.

The qualifying program is the social contract: you keep the perks as long as you keep contributing.

What this is NOT

This isn't busy-work. We don't expect Founding Partners to hit some arbitrary metric. The activities are things you'd already do if you were active in the cooperative — speaking at events, mentoring new folks, attending summits. The qualifying program just makes sure Founding 25 status stays meaningful: it represents real ongoing contribution to the network.

FAQ

How is qualifying tracked?

Each activity is logged in your Partner dashboard with date, type, and credit count. Founding 25 desk reviews annually each December. You receive a credit summary every January.

Can I "double-up" on activities to bank credits?

No banking. Each calendar year stands alone. You need 3 credits IN that year. Excess credits don't roll forward.

What if I'm sick / on leave / have a family emergency?

Reach out to the Founding 25 desk at support@getlymx.com. We grant exception years case-by-case. The grace path also gives you 2 cushion years before any actual penalty.

Can I substitute activities not on the list?

Yes — write to the Founding 25 desk with what you're proposing. Things that have counted: writing a published piece about LYMX in trade press, hosting a podcast guest spot, recruiting a marquee enterprise prospect.

If I lose Founding 25 status, do I lose past commissions?

No — past commissions stay paid at the rates they were earned. Going forward, only NEW activations are at the standard rate.

Are activities cooperative — do I need permission?

Most are self-directed (speaking at an event, contributing playbook content). A few — co-hosting events with venue reimbursement, mentor pairing — go through the Founding 25 desk so we can pair you well + cover costs.

Can a Founding Partner who later becomes a Master Partner also qualify?

Yes — Founding 25 status and Master Partner tier are independent. You can be both.

What if the qualifying program rules change later?

Locked at the rules in effect when you earned Founding 25 status. New rules apply only to NEW Founding Partners (if a future cohort exists). This is part of the grandfather promise.
