# Use the Income Projector to plan your income

The projector at `getlymx.com/projection.html` isn't just a calculator — it's a planning tool that answers one question: **given your skills and effort, where should you focus to earn the most?** Everyone's path is different. Here's how to use each part.

## 1 · See where you stand today

If you're signed in, the top of the page shows your **actual** lifetime cash, LYMX, and pending payout (from your real ledger), plus a **You vs the network** panel. The black tick on each bar is the network average. Wherever your bar is short of the tick, that's a lever you can pull:

- **Activations below average?** Recruiting more businesses is your fastest win.
- **Team partners below average?** Building a team is your biggest untapped lever.
- **LYMX per business below average?** Recruit higher-volume types (cafés, fast food) or help your current businesses drive more activity — it lifts every override you already earn.

## 2 · Model what you'll recruit

In **Recruit businesses**, add how many of each category you plan to bring on. Each type earns LYMX differently — a café does far more transactions than a salon, so it generates more LYMX commission even though the cash override is the same. Tap **edit** on any category to tune the monthly LYMX volume to what you actually see in your market (the defaults are estimates).

## 3 · Model your team

In **Build your team**, set how many partners you have at each generation (G1 = people you recruited, G2 = their recruits, G3 = one deeper) and the average businesses each brings. You earn 3% / 2% / 1% overrides on what they activate — income that grows without you doing the selling.

## 4 · Read the results

The right side shows your projected **monthly cash + LYMX**, the annualized figure, and the **one-time sign-up bonuses** you'd earn building it. "Where the income comes from" breaks it down by source so you can see what's driving it.

## 5 · Find your next best move

**Where to focus next** ranks your highest-yield next action given your current plan — recruit one more café vs coach a G1 partner vs recruit a new partner — by the monthly cash each adds. The ⭐ is your best move right now.

## 6 · Hunter vs Builder

Same weekly effort, two styles, 12 months out:

- **🎯 Hunter** — you recruit businesses yourself. Sign-up bonuses pay the fastest first dollar.
- **🌳 Builder** — you recruit partners who recruit. Smaller early, but it compounds and becomes passive.

Pick the one that fits your strengths — there's no wrong path, just different ones.

## 7 · Set a goal and let it remind you

Enter your monthly cash target in the **Goal planner** and hit **Plan it**. It uses your network gaps to tell you the fastest route — how many businesses to recruit, or how many partners to build — split into an active (sign-up bonus) path and a passive (recurring overrides) path. Hit **★ Set as my goal** to save it: a progress **meter** then shows how this month is tracking, and your **daily income digest** will nudge you toward it each morning.

## Common questions

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Why is my monthly cash from overrides so small? | Recurring override is ~9% of a $199 subscription ≈ $18/business/mo. The big cash is the **$500 sign-up bonus** per activation. That's why "Hunter" pays fast — keep recruiting and the bonuses stack. |
| Is the LYMX I see real money? | No — LYMX commission is spendable across the partner network, not convertible to cash. Cash = sign-up bonuses + subscription overrides. The projector keeps them separate on purpose. |
| Are the category LYMX numbers exact? | They're editable estimates. Tune them to your market with the **edit** link; your projections update live. |
| Why don't my numbers match the comp page? | The projector defaults to the **standard** partner rate. Founding-25 partners earn a higher direct rate + the $1,000 speed bonus — see the Comp Plan playbook. |
