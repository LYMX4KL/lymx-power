About

Brew & Bean is a neighborhood specialty coffee shop and bakery in the heart of Midtown. We roast small-batch beans from a family farm in Costa Rica, bake everything from scratch every morning, and run a sunny patio that's perfect for laptops, dates, or solo afternoons. Family-owned since 2019.

We were one of the first Las Vegas businesses to join LYMX. Earn 5 LYMX per $1 spent — coffee, pastries, lunch, and our small retail shelf are all eligible. Redeem any LYMX balance toward your order, no minimums.
