🤖 Android — using Chrome

1

Open Chrome (or Brave, Edge, Samsung Internet — any modern browser)

Most Android browsers support PWA install. Chrome is the most reliable. If you don't see the install option in your default browser, try Chrome.

2

Go to wallet.lymx.com

Type into the address bar. You may see a small banner at the bottom asking "Install LYMX?" — if so, you can tap that and skip ahead to step 5.

3

Tap the three dots menu (top-right)

The standard Chrome menu icon. Looks like ⋮ in the top right of the browser.

4

Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"

The wording varies by Android version. Look for either "Install app" (newer Chrome) or "Add to Home screen" (older). Both do the same thing.

5

Confirm the name and tap Install

You'll see a preview of the LYMX icon. Tap Install. Some Android versions also let you choose where on the home screen.

6

LYMX is now on your home screen

The icon appears in your app drawer + home screen. Tap to open. Behaves like a real app — full-screen, fast, no browser chrome.

FAQ

Why isn't the install option showing up on my phone?

Three common causes: (1) you're using Chrome on iOS — switch to Safari for iOS PWA install. (2) Your browser is too old — update to latest version. (3) You're in Private/Incognito mode — exit and try again in a regular tab.

Will I lose my balance when I install?

No — install is just a shortcut, no data changes. Your wallet, balance, history all stay intact. The install simply makes the app accessible from your home screen.

Do I need a password every time I open the wallet?

You'll set a 4-digit PIN. After that, the wallet auto-logs-in on your phone. PIN is required only for redemption (security). Other browsing is one-tap.

What if I get a new phone — does the install transfer?

You'll need to re-install on the new phone (same 6-step process). Your balance + history follow your phone number, not the device. See account recovery.

Is this an "app"? Why don't you have one in the App Store?

It's a Progressive Web App (PWA) — modern web technology that behaves like an app without needing the App Store. We chose this on purpose: faster updates, no review delays, works on any phone, no download. Same UX as a native app, simpler maintenance.

What if my phone is older / doesn't support PWAs?

Older iPhones (iOS 12 and below) and old Android (8 and below) may not have full PWA support. The wallet still works in any modern browser — bookmark wallet.lymx.com instead. Just won't have the home-screen icon.

Can I install LYMX on my tablet too?

Yes — same instructions. iPads use Safari + Add to Home Screen. Android tablets use Chrome + Install app. Works identically.
