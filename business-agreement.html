1. Enrollment & activation

You enroll by completing the LYMX onboarding flow at /onboarding and agreeing to this Agreement. Activation occurs when LYMX accepts your application and sends an activation confirmation. You authorize the listed payment method to be charged the $850 sign-up fee on activation.

2. Fees, subscription & what's included

Sign-up fee — $850 (one-time)

Includes:

  • The first 3 calendar months of Enterprise service (worth $597)
  • Top-of-homepage feature spot on getlymx.com for the first 30 days post-activation
  • One-time launch email blast announcing your Business to all existing LYMX customers in your metro
  • Activation kit: branded LYMX QR signage, listing setup on getlymx.com, 30-minute onboarding call with the launch team, custom welcome promo for your first 100 customers

Monthly subscription — $199 from month 4

Beginning the 4th calendar month after activation, you will be charged $199 on the same calendar day each month. The subscription includes ongoing access to the LYMX network, the Business POS and dashboard, settlement processing, and unlimited transaction volume up to 3,000 orders per month. Orders beyond 3,000/mo are billed at 15 LYMX each (paid in your settlement statement).

Payment

You authorize LYMX to charge the payment method on file for the sign-up fee, monthly subscription, and any net amount owed in settlement. If a payment fails, we may pause your account and retry. You're responsible for keeping your payment method current.

3. Issuance rate & promotional flexibility

Your base issuance rate (the number of LYMX units you issue per dollar of customer purchase, e.g., 5 LYMX/$1) is set by LYMX at activation. The base rate is locked for the contract term — you may not unilaterally change it.

Within the locked base rate, you may run platform-approved promotional campaigns of limited scope and duration:

  • Time-bound rate multipliers (e.g., happy hour, day-of-week, weekly recurring)
  • Item-specific multipliers (e.g., 2× LYMX on a specific menu item this week)
  • First-time-customer multipliers
  • One-time event promotions (e.g., holiday-day specials)

Promotions must have explicit start and end conditions and be configured through the LYMX promotions dashboard. Promotions are reflected in the LYMX units you purchase from LYMX in the relevant settlement period.

4. Buy/sell mechanic with LYMX

Issuance — you purchase units from LYMX

When you issue LYMX units to a customer at the point of sale, you are purchasing those units from LYMX at a wholesale price of $0.008 per unit (eighty percent of the unit's face value of $0.01). LYMX bills you for these unit purchases in the monthly settlement statement.

Redemption — LYMX buys units back from you

When a customer presents previously-earned LYMX units to you as payment, you accept those units at face value as payment from the customer (1 unit = $0.01 of payment). LYMX simultaneously buys those units back from you at the same wholesale price of $0.008 per unit. The 20% gap between face value ($0.01) and our buy-back price ($0.008) is absorbed by you as the discount you offer the customer the network referred to you.

The math on a single LYMX unit

  • Face value when accepted as payment: $0.01
  • Our wholesale purchase price (issuer pays us): $0.008
  • Our wholesale buy-back price (we pay redeemer): $0.008
  • The 20% gap on redemption: absorbed by the redeeming Business as the discount given to the customer

LYMX is the sole issuer, seller, and buyer-back of LYMX units. We do not act as escrow agent or money transmitter. The buy/sell relationship is structured as ordinary accounts payable / receivable between LYMX and your Business.

5. Monthly settlement

On the 1st business day of each calendar month, LYMX produces a settlement statement covering the prior calendar month. The statement includes:

  • Unit purchases: total units you purchased from LYMX during the period × $0.008
  • Unit buy-backs: total units LYMX bought back from you × $0.008
  • Net: purchases minus buy-backs (positive = you owe LYMX; negative = LYMX owes you)
  • Subscription & overage: $199 monthly subscription, plus any order-overage charges

Net settlement is debited from or credited to your payment method on file within 5 business days. You may export your settlement data as CSV for your accounting system at any time.

Customer reviews appear on your listing. LYMX moderates reviews under the policy described in the Terms of Service. You may report reviews you believe are inaccurate or unfair via your dashboard; LYMX reviews flagged content within 7 business days.

8. Conduct standards

To maintain the network's integrity, you agree:

  • To honor LYMX redemption from any holder presenting a valid LYMX QR code or balance, up to your stated daily limits

9. Term, suspension & termination

This Agreement is month-to-month with no minimum term beyond the 3 months covered by your sign-up fee. Either party may cancel at any time for any reason or no reason, with reasonable advance notice when possible.

LYMX may suspend or terminate your account immediately for violation of this Agreement, the Terms of Service, applicable law, or in cases of suspected fraud or imminent harm.

10. Post-cancellation wind-down

When you submit cancellation:

  • Issuance stops immediately. The moment cancellation is submitted, your outstanding LYMX balance is auto-capped — you cannot issue new LYMX during the wind-down period.
  • Listing flag. Your getlymx.com listing displays a "Leaving the LYMX network — still accepting LYMX" banner with days-remaining countdown. The "Earn LYMX here" badge is removed.
  • Customer-facing balance. Customers can see the remaining capacity at your Business (% of outstanding balance still redeemable), so they can spend their LYMX before you exit.
  • 90-day acceptance window. You continue to accept (redeem) LYMX from customers for 90 days after cancellation OR until your outstanding balance reaches $0 — whichever comes first. Settlement on each redemption follows the 80% redemption rule.
  • After 90 days: your listing is removed from the public getlymx.com directory. You may continue accepting LYMX privately until balance reaches $0 (we don't strand customers).
  • Final settlement issued at the end of the wind-down. Any prepaid subscription beyond cancellation is pro-rated and refunded.

10b. Selling your Business — asset transfer

Different from cancellation. If you're selling the Business:

  • The LYMX account transfers to the new owner as part of the asset sale.
  • The new owner inherits: account history, customer relationships, current balance, public listing, Partner attribution.
  • Condition: the new owner must accept this Agreement and stay in the LYMX network. If they decline, the standard 90-day wind-down (section 10) applies from the date the sale closes.
  • Notify LYMX at hello@getlymx.com at least 30 days before close. We'll issue a transfer-of-ownership addendum for both parties to sign.
  • Your Partner attribution stays with the account — the originating Partner continues to earn ongoing override commissions on the account post-transfer (subject to standard commission rules).
