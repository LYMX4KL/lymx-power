How to connect Toast

Step-by-step setup. ⏱️ Total: ~18 min

1

Open Integrations in your LYMX dashboard

From your Business dashboard, click Settings → Integrations. Find Toast and click Connect.

~30 sec
2

Sign in to Toast Web (admin account required)

Toast OAuth opens. You'll need Restaurant Admin permissions to authorize integrations. If you don't have that, ask your operator. Toast's permission scope: read menu, read transactions, read locations.

~3 min
3

Pick locations to sync

If you operate multiple restaurants, choose which to sync. Most owners select all — but you may want to test on one location first. You can change this later.

~1 min
4

Configure ticket-line LYMX behavior

Two restaurant-specific decisions. Per-course or per-check awarding? Per-course is the default (better for higher engagement). Tip handling? LYMX awards on pre-tip subtotal by default. You can adjust either.

~3 min
5

Map menu categories to LYMX multipliers

Toast menus often have detailed category structures. Walk through and confirm or set the multiplier per category (default 1×). For higher-margin items (cocktails, desserts), boost to 2× to drive attach.

~6 min
6

Test with a $1 ticket

Ring up a test guest check. Confirm the LYMX issuance flows through within 30 sec. Refund the test ticket — confirm the LYMX issuance reverses automatically.

~2 min
7

Train your servers (5-min video)

Servers' new flow: ask guests if they have LYMX → if yes, prompt them at the table-side mobile pay screen. Guests apply directly from their phone. Watch the server training video.

~5 min

Frequently asked questions

Does this work with Toast Pay (table-side mobile pay)?

Yes. The LYMX option appears in the Toast Pay flow without server intervention. Guests can scan their LYMX QR or apply LYMX from their phone before paying.

Does it work with Toast for online ordering?

Yes. Online orders sync the same way as in-house. Guests enter their LYMX wallet phone number at checkout. We tested this extensively with Oakline Kitchen.

What about delivery (DoorDash, UberEats integrations through Toast)?

Third-party delivery transactions don't sync to LYMX by default — they're considered third-party orders. If a guest orders through DoorDash, LYMX isn't awarded. Only direct in-house and Toast online orders earn LYMX.

How does it handle tips?

By default, LYMX is awarded on the pre-tip subtotal. The reasoning: tips go to your servers, not the restaurant — so LYMX awarded on tips would inflate the restaurant's LYMX cost without being a true sale. You can adjust this in Settings if you prefer post-tip.

Multi-location: does each restaurant get separate LYMX accounting?

Yes. Each location is its own LYMX entity for billing & settlement, but shares menu data + branding from the parent. Reports can be viewed per-location or rolled up.

Does this affect my Toast subscription tier?

No. The LYMX integration uses Toast's API, which is included in all paid Toast plans. No tier upgrade needed.
