Xero vs QuickBooks?

If you're already on Xero, this is your setup. If you're on QuickBooks, see the QuickBooks integration. The LYMX outputs are similar — but Xero handles multi-currency natively, which matters if your Business has international ops.

🌍 Multi-currency Businesses

If you operate in USD but your Xero books are in CAD, AUD, GBP, etc. — Xero's native currency conversion handles this. LYMX posts entries in USD; Xero converts on the fly. Note: LYMX is US-only currently. If you have non-US locations, those aren't part of the LYMX network yet.

How to connect Xero

One-time setup. ⏱️ Total: ~10 min

1

Open Integrations in your LYMX dashboard

From your Business dashboard, click Settings → Integrations. Find Xero and click Connect.

~30 sec
2

Authorize via Xero OAuth

Sign in to Xero. Choose which Xero organization to connect (if you have multiple). Authorize LYMX to write bills + sales entries.

~2 min
3

Map LYMX items to your Xero chart of accounts

Confirm or adjust mappings. Xero defaults: LYMX wholesale → "Cost of Goods Sold" or "Marketing." Subscription → "Software Subscriptions." Sign-up fee → "Marketing & Promotion" (your choice — same as QuickBooks).

~4 min
4

Set up GST/VAT codes (international Businesses only)

If you're in AU, NZ, UK, or EU, Xero will ask which GST/VAT codes to apply to LYMX entries. Default: same as your Software Subscriptions account.

~2 min
5

Run a backfill (optional)

Backfill the past 90 days of LYMX activity into Xero. Useful for cleaning up your books. 2-3 min in the background.

~2 min
6

Verify in Xero

Open Xero → Reports → Account Transactions. Filter for "LYMX" — entries should appear with the correct categorization.

~1 min

Frequently asked questions

I'm in Australia / NZ / UK — does this work?

Yes for the Xero side — the integration handles GST/VAT correctly for AU, NZ, UK, EU. However, LYMX itself is currently US-only. So this integration is most useful if you operate in the US but use Xero for accounting.

Does this work with Xero Standard, Premium, Ultimate?

All Xero plans that include the API. Xero Cashbook (the basic plan) doesn't include the API, so you'd need to upgrade to Standard.

What about multi-organization Xero accounts (advisor accounts)?

Yes. If you're an accountant with multiple client orgs, you can install LYMX integration once and select which client org to sync. We can also enable multi-org sync if you have multi-Business clients on LYMX.

Is there a fee for the Xero integration?

No. The Xero integration is included in your LYMX Business subscription. Xero charges its own subscription separately (which is independent of us).

Can my accountant set this up for me?

Yes. Add your accountant as a Xero advisor user, give them access to your LYMX Business account, and they can do the setup. Most do this in 10–15 min.

What if I want to switch from QuickBooks to Xero?

Disconnect QuickBooks integration first (Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks → Disconnect). Then connect Xero. Your historical LYMX data stays preserved. Future entries go to Xero.
