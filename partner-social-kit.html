The 1-line disclosure

Drop this somewhere in every post about LYMX — caption, comment, or story sticker. Required.

"I'm a LYMX Partner — I get paid if you sign up through my link."

The three post styles that work

  • The Business owner story. Feature a real Business — what changed for them after joining. Specific, concrete, narrative.
  • The customer pain story. Tell the airline-points / fast-food-app frustration that LYMX solves. Don't name brands by name.
  • The behind-the-scenes. What you did this week, who you talked to, what you learned. Builds your personal credibility.

Don't do these

  • Don't promise income amounts ("I made $5K this month!"). The Income Disclosure must accompany any earnings claim.
  • Don't use "passive income" or "guaranteed" or anything close.
  • Don't @ -mention Businesses on LYMX without checking with them first.
  • Don't run paid ads with your Partner link without legal sign-off.
