What we learned from the Founding 25

🤝Density beats reach

Eight Businesses concentrated in Midtown drove more network referrals than 30 spread across the metro. Geographic density compounds. We're using this for Phoenix & Reno launches.

⏱️Network effect kicks in at week 3–4

The first 2–3 weeks felt like a regular loyalty program. By week 4, redemptions from other Businesses started appearing. Weeks 5–8 they ramped sharply.

🔁Day-of-week patterns are real

Weekday daytime LYMX activity was driven by office-worker patterns. Weekend activity skewed retail/spa/restaurant. Most Founding Businesses now run different multipliers on different days.

📈Owner cadence matters

The Businesses with weekly 30-min review habits (5 of 8) outperformed the others on every metric. Setup is cheap; ongoing engagement is the differentiator.
