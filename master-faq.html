For customers

12 questions
What is LYMX in one sentence?

LYMX is a shared loyalty network for local Businesses — earn LYMX at one Business, spend it at any other in the network. More →

What is 1 LYMX worth?

1 LYMX = $0.01 of spending power, always. 1,000 LYMX = $10 you can apply to any LYMX Business's bill.

Does LYMX expire?

Never. No expiration, no inactivity penalty. Earn it today, spend it next year.

Do I need to download an app?

No. The LYMX wallet runs in your browser. Add it to your home screen for app-like access.

How do I pay with LYMX at a Business?

Open your wallet → tap "Pay with QR" → show the QR to the cashier. They scan it on their iPad. Done. Full step-by-step →

Can I get cash for my LYMX?

No. LYMX is a promotional credit, not money. It's spendable at LYMX Businesses but not redeemable for cash. More on what LYMX is and isn't →

What's the welcome bonus when I sign up?

100 LYMX (≈ $1) automatically credited to your wallet at sign-up. No purchase required.

How does refer-a-friend work?

Share your personal link from customer-referral. When a friend signs up + earns their first LYMX, you both get 200 LYMX. No cap.

What if I don't recognize a transaction in my wallet?

Open the transaction → tap "Report an issue" → file a dispute. We respond within 1 business day. If you suspect fraud, hit "Lock my wallet now" first — your balance is safe while we investigate.

Can I use LYMX in cities outside Las Vegas?

Right now LYMX is Las Vegas-only. Phase 2 (Q2 2026) adds Henderson + Summerlin. Phase 3 (2027) is multi-city expansion: San Diego, Phoenix, Denver, Austin, Salt Lake. Coverage map →

What if I lose my phone?

Sign in to your wallet on a new device — your balance and transaction history follow your phone number. If a stranger has access to your phone, hit "Lock my wallet" via the dispute flow or call 702-555-LYMX.

Where can I see all my activity?

The My history page in your wallet shows earnings + spending grouped by Business, with totals and your "favorite Business" detection.

For Businesses

11 questions
What does LYMX cost a Business?

$850 one-time activation (includes 3 months free + Founding-25 perks while available) + $200/month after that. Locked for the contract term. Pricing →

How long is the contract?

12 months initial, then month-to-month with 30-day cancel. Full agreement →

Do I need to replace my POS?

No. The LYMX POS runs as a Web App on an iPad you already own. Square / Toast / Clover / Shopify integrations available — see integrations.

How does the buy/sell mechanic work financially?

You buy LYMX units from us at $0.008 per LYMX (80% of face value) to issue to customers. When customers redeem at any participating Business, that Business sells the redeemed units back to us at the same $0.008. The 20% gap is the redeeming Business's effective discount.

How does sales tax work when a customer pays with LYMX?

Sales tax is collected and remitted on the full ticket value (not the LYMX-discounted amount). Your POS shows the full ticket; LYMX is treated as a discount line above the tax line. More →

What if my employee makes an error in a transaction?

Two paths: void within 60 seconds (no extra steps), or file a settlement dispute for older errors. Disputes resolve in 1-2 business days.

Can I run promotions like Happy Hour multipliers?

Yes. Schedule time-bound (e.g., 2× LYMX 4-6 PM Saturdays), item-targeted (only on espresso), or first-time-customer multipliers. Promotion engine →

How do monthly settlements work?

Net of unit purchases vs. buy-backs is settled via ACH on the 5th business day of each month. QuickBooks CSV export included. More →

How do I refer another Business?

Use your personal link from biz-refer. When a Business activates through your link, you get $200 cash + they get $200 LYMX welcome bonus on top of standard package.

I have multiple locations. Is there a chain plan?

Yes. LYMX Enterprise covers 3+ locations: from $150/loc/mo (volume tiers go to $140/loc/mo at 25+), single MSA + billing, dedicated CSM, 99.9% uptime SLA.

What if I want to cancel?

30-day written notice after the initial 12-month term. We'll process up to 90 days of customer LYMX redemptions during the wind-down. Final settlement issued for any remaining net buy-back balance. Full wind-down terms →

For Partners (recruiters)

8 questions
How does Partner compensation work?

Two layers: Production = $500 sign-up bonus + 9% recurring override on Businesses you personally recruit. Generations = 3% override on G1 Partners' production, 2% on G2, 1% on G3. Full agreement →

Do I have to buy anything to be a Partner?

No. No purchase required. The Partner program is structured to comply with NRS 207.235 anti-pyramid law and FTC guidance. You get paid only on bona-fide third-party Business sign-ups.

How and when do I get paid?

Sign-up bonuses ($500): 30 days after Business activates (anti-fraud window). Recurring overrides: monthly ACH on 5th business day. 1099-NEC issued annually. Payouts →

What's the tier ladder?

Bronze (5 Biz) → Silver (15) → Gold (30) → Platinum (50) → Master Partner (special invite). Each tier unlocks higher overrides + recognition. Tier dashboard →

What happens if a Business cancels in the first 30 days?

The $500 sign-up bonus is clawed back. We pay sign-up bonuses on day 30 specifically to avoid this scenario where possible.

Can I switch sponsors after I sign up?

Within 14 days of signing, if no commissions have been earned, sponsor change is reviewed case-by-case. After 14 days, locked. Policy details →

What's the deal with FTC compliance?

Every public mention of LYMX must include the disclosure: "I'm a LYMX Partner — I get paid if you sign up through my link." Required by FTC for any compensated promotion. Compliance training →

What's typical earning?

Median active Partner earned $540/month in our first reporting period. Top Partner (Sienna): $11,840/month after 9 months. Earnings vary substantially by individual effort. Transparency report →

Legal & data

6 questions
Does LYMX sell my data?

No. We don't sell, lease, or share customer data with marketers, ad networks, data brokers, or AI training services. Full privacy policy →

Does LYMX use my data to train AI?

No. Every AI provider we use (Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepL) has a no-training-on-our-data contractual commitment. Full AI policy →

How can I download or delete my data?

Self-service at data-export. Pick format (JSON / CSV / PDF), categories, and request export — usually ready in 90 seconds. Delete account: 30 days production, 90 days backups, 7-day undo window.

What's LYMX's regulatory status?

LYMX is a promotional credit, not money or stored value. Outside Nevada NRS 671 money-transmitter scope. Outside NRS 598B gift-certificate rules (no expiration). Full positioning →

Has LYMX had any data breaches?

Zero data breaches since launch. One scheduled maintenance incident (March 2026, 22 min, no data affected). Full incident archive: incident-history.

What about accessibility (WCAG)?

Targeting WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Most principles met today; "Robust" in progress. Independent audit targeted Q4 2026. Accessibility statement →
