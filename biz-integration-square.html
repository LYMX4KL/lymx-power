How to connect Square

Step-by-step setup. ⏱️ Total: ~12 min

1

Open the Integrations page in your LYMX dashboard

From your Business dashboard, click Settings → Integrations. Find the Square card and click Connect.

~30 sec
2

Sign in to Square & authorize LYMX

A Square OAuth window opens. Sign in with your Square admin credentials. Review the requested permissions (read menu, read/write transactions, read customers). Click Authorize.

~2 min
3

Choose which Square locations to sync

If you have multiple Square locations, pick the ones to sync. Most Businesses select all. You can change this later under Integrations → Square → Locations.

~1 min
4

Map your menu categories

LYMX shows your Square menu. For each category, confirm or set the LYMX multiplier (default 1×). You can change individual items in Menu later.

~5 min
5

Run a test transaction

In Square, ring up a $1 test sale. The LYMX dashboard should show the issuance within 30 seconds — 5 LYMX awarded. If you don't see it, click Re-sync in Integrations.

~2 min
6

Train your cashiers (5-min video)

The new flow at checkout: ask the customer if they have LYMX → if yes, scan their QR → ring up Square as normal. LYMX auto-issues. Watch the cashier training video.

~5 min

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to replace my Square POS?

No. LYMX layers on top — Square remains your point of sale, payments processor, and inventory tool. We just talk to Square via its API.

What happens to my existing Square Loyalty stamps?

They stay in Square. We don't migrate them automatically — that's your call. Some Businesses run both side-by-side for a transition period, then turn off Square Loyalty after 30–60 days. Others turn it off immediately and offer existing stamp customers a one-time LYMX bonus.

What if Square has a data outage?

If Square's API is down, we queue any pending syncs and replay them once Square comes back up. LYMX issuances captured during the outage period are reconciled within 30 minutes of recovery. Customer-facing wallet balances continue to work normally — they don't depend on Square.

Can I disconnect later?

Yes. Click Integrations → Square → Disconnect. Your menu, customer, and historical transaction data stays in LYMX (we don't delete it). Future Square sales just won't auto-issue LYMX until you re-connect.

Does LYMX see customer credit card data?

No. We never see card numbers, CVCs, or any PCI-scope data. Square handles all of that. LYMX only receives transaction metadata: total amount, item names, customer ID (if you have one). See our security overview for details.

What about Square Online sales?

Online sales sync the same way as in-store. Customer can earn LYMX online → redeem in-store. Or vice versa. The customer just enters their LYMX wallet phone number at checkout.
