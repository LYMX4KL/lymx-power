What we learned from talking to customers

🌎

The discovery effect is bigger than the savings

Most customers say their favorite thing about LYMX isn't the money saved — it's that they ended up trying Businesses they wouldn't have otherwise. Three new restaurants in 6 months is common.

🎁

People treat LYMX as "found money"

Customers tend to redeem LYMX on small treats they wouldn't normally buy — an extra croissant, a movie at the indie theater, a candle from the gift shop. It's mental-accounting magic.

📱

The "no app" thing actually matters

Roughly a third of customers cited "I didn't have to download anything" as a reason they signed up. Most loyalty programs lose people at the install step. Web-based wallet skips it.
