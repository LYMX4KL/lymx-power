From zero to your first LYMX in 5 minutes

No app to download. Nothing to set up upfront. Your first LYMX-earning purchase will walk you through the rest. This page just shows you what to expect.

5 min
From walking in to earned LYMX
If you've ever joined a coffee shop's punch-card program, you already know how this works. Just simpler — and one wallet works at every Business in the LYMX network.
1

Walk into a LYMX Business

Look for the gold "We accept LYMX" decal on the door, or check the network map before you go. Every Business in the network earns LYMX on every purchase — restaurants, retail, salons, services, you name it.

Tip: Vegas locals — there are 218 Businesses already on the network as of August 2026. Find your favorite local spot first.
2

Pay like normal

Card, cash, Apple Pay — doesn't matter. LYMX layers on top of how you already pay. The Business will ask "Are you on LYMX?" or "Do you want to start a LYMX wallet?" That's your cue.

  • If yes — show the QR they scan (or give them your phone number)
  • If first-timer — they text you a link to set up your wallet (10 seconds)
3

Open the link, set up your wallet

The link is just a web page — no app store, no sign-up form. Just enter your name and a 4-digit PIN. The wallet remembers your phone, so next time you tap the link, you're already in.

Privacy: We don't ask for email, address, or birthday at sign-up. Just enough to keep your wallet secure.
4

Watch your first LYMX appear

The Business has already entered your purchase into their POS. By the time you've finished setting up, your LYMX is in the wallet. Standard rate is 5 LYMX per dollar spent — so a $20 lunch = 100 LYMX.

  • The exact rate depends on the Business — they set it (commonly 5/$1 or 3/$1)
  • You'll see the rate on every receipt
5

Spend LYMX anywhere on the network

Here's where it gets different from a punch card. The LYMX you earned at the coffee shop? You can spend at the salon. Or the dim sum place. Or any other LYMX Business. One wallet, the entire network. Show your QR at checkout, the cashier scans, you redeem.

100 LYMX = $1. Simple math. No expiration, no blackout dates, no minimums.
6

(Optional) Pin the wallet to your home screen

If you want one-tap access, open wallet.lymx.com in Safari or Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen." Now it acts like an app, but it's still just a web page. No download, no updates.

  • iOS: Share button → Add to Home Screen
  • Android: ⋮ menu → Install app

That's it. You're on the network.

From here, every LYMX Business in Las Vegas earns you LYMX, and your balance spends anywhere on the network. Welcome.

Common first-timer questions

Do I have to pay anything to join LYMX?

No. Customer wallets are free forever. The Businesses pay the platform fee — you just earn and spend.

What if I lose my phone?

See account recovery. Short version: your LYMX is tied to your phone number, not the device. We'll get you back in.

Does my LYMX expire?

No — LYMX has no expiration as long as you make at least one transaction every 24 months. Inactive wallets after 24 months may be archived (you can recover them).

Can I share my balance with my spouse / family?

Each wallet is tied to one phone number, but you can send LYMX between wallets if you want to share with a family member. Learn more.

What if a Business doesn't have my favorite restaurant on the network?

You can refer them — every successful Business referral earns you a 500 LYMX bonus. Refer a Business.

Is there a tier system or status level?

Not right now. Every customer earns the same rate at the same Business. We may introduce status perks later, but the core promise is "one rate, one wallet, no fine print."
