!Lowlights & what we're doing about them

  • Nevada money-transmitter counsel review took 6 weeks longer than planned. Counsel needed deeper review of the buy/sell mechanic given the novel structure. Outcome was the right one (clear opinion that LYMX is outside NRS 671), but it pushed our marketing campaign into Q2.
  • Customer-buyback exclusion confused 2 prospects. Some customers initially asked "can I cash out my LYMX?" We've added clearer disclaimers across the wallet, signup flow, and homepage. Down to 1 confused inquiry per 100 sign-ups now.
  • POS integration with Square is taking longer than expected. Their API rate-limits are stricter than docs suggested. We're reverting to a receipt-scan fallback for Square Businesses while the integration is finished. 4 of our 13 Businesses on Square are using the fallback today; not blocking.
  • Two Launch 25 Businesses churned. One was a clear product fit issue (B2B-only nail studio with too few walk-ins). One was personal — owner sold the business. We replaced both within 2 weeks. Lesson: tightened our intake criteria for the next cohort.
