Why integrate Google Business Profile

Most local-search traffic happens on Google Maps and Google search before customers ever reach your storefront. The LYMX integration makes your Google presence work harder — auto-posting promos, surfacing the LYMX badge in search results, and pulling Google reviews into your LYMX listing for cross-validation.

How to connect Google Business Profile

One-time setup. ⏱️ Total: ~10 min

1

You need a verified Google Business Profile

If you don't have one yet, set it up first at business.google.com. Verification typically takes 1–14 days (Google sends a postcard to your address). You must be the listed owner or manager.

Prerequisite
2

Open Integrations in your LYMX dashboard

From your Business dashboard, click Settings → Integrations. Find Google Business Profile and click Connect.

~30 sec
3

Authorize Google OAuth

Sign in to your Google account with admin/owner permissions on your Business Profile. Approve the requested scopes: read profile, write posts, read reviews. Approve.

~2 min
4

Pick which Google Profile to link (if multiple)

If you manage multiple Google Business Profiles (multi-location chains, or a personal profile + business profile), choose the one matching this LYMX Business.

~1 min
5

Configure auto-post settings

Pick which LYMX events trigger Google Posts: multiplier promos (recommended), monthly milestones (optional), seasonal events (optional). You can override per-post.

~3 min
6

Verify the integration

Run a test promo (set a 1-day 2× multiplier on any item). Within 5 min, check your Google Business Profile — the post should appear there. If not, check the Integration log in LYMX dashboard.

~3 min

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between this and just running Google Ads?

Google Ads costs money per click. Google Business Profile is free — and the integration just makes it more active and visible. We're not buying placement; we're amplifying what Google already gives you for free.

Does this work with multi-location?

Yes. If you manage multiple Google Profiles (one per location), you can link each to its corresponding LYMX location. Multi-location LYMX accounts get a "rollup view" of Google insights across all locations.

Can I disable the auto-posting?

Yes — anytime. Settings → Integrations → Google → toggle "Auto-post" off. The integration still pulls reviews + insights inbound; just no outbound posting.

What if I don't have a Google Business Profile yet?

Set one up first — it's free, and required by Google to be verified at the physical address you operate from. Typical setup + verification: 1–14 days. We can guide you through this during your LYMX onboarding.

Does this affect my Google ranking?

Maybe — Google's local ranking is a black box. Anecdotally, LYMX Businesses with this integration see a ~12% lift in "near me" search ranking versus baseline. We can't promise this, but the increased Posts activity is a known positive ranking signal.

What about Google Maps + Apple Maps?

Google Maps integration is what's described here. Apple Maps doesn't have a comparable API for third-party apps yet. Apple Maps does pull from your Google Profile in some cases (via Yelp partnerships), so this integration may indirectly help Apple Maps too.
