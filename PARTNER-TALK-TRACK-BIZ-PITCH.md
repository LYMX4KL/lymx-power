# Partner Talk-Track — "We Pay You to Accept Our Customers"

> **For:** Helen, Rachel, and any future Partner doing in-person business recruitment.
> **Use:** Door-knocking, walk-ins, coffee meetings, networking events.
> **Length:** This entire script should take ~3 minutes to deliver before they say yes/no/maybe.
> **Drafted:** 2026-05-21

---

## The 15-second opener (memorize this exactly)

> **"Hi, I'm [name]. I work with a loyalty network called LYMX, and I have a weird question — would you rather (a) keep running loyalty programs that cost you money, or (b) get paid every time a customer redeems a reward at your store?"**

That's it. **Stop. Wait for them to answer.** Most people will say "(b), what's the catch?" Now you have permission to keep going.

---

## The 60-second walkthrough

> **"Here's how LYMX works in three sentences:**
>
> **One — customers earn LYMX rewards at any business in our network.**
>
> **Two — when they redeem those rewards at your store, you give them the discount — and LYMX pays you cash for the redemption amount, on the spot. Not the customer. Us.**
>
> **Three — we're hand-picking 25 Founding Businesses to seed our network in [their area]. Three months free, $199 a month after, $850 setup. You also get a permanent feature spot on our app's homepage."**

Then pause. Let them ask the next question.

## The number-drop (when they ask "how many customers?")

> **"Right now there are [LIVE_NUM — pull live from getlymx.com/business] customers in our network sitting on about $[LIVE_USD] in rewards. They want to spend them. We have one Founding Business signed up so far — so anyone who joins in the next few weeks is going to be the only place those customers can redeem locally."**

Lean into the SMALL number — frame it as **first-mover advantage**, not lack of traction:

> **"Yes, we're early. That's why the 25 spots come with three months free and the permanent feature spot. By the time we hit 500 customers, those slots will be gone."**

## Objection handling (5 most common)

### "How do I know your customers are real?"
> "Fair question. Every LYMX reward is issued from a verified transaction at another business in our network — we audit issuances in the ledger, and you can see the source. No bot accounts, no fake numbers. The live counter on our /business page pulls straight from the database."

### "What's the catch on the $199?"
> "There's no catch — that's the cost of running the platform for you. Payments, fraud monitoring, the customer wallet, the redemption rails. You pay $0 ad spend, $0 customer-acquisition cost, $0 program design. The $199 covers the infrastructure. If you'd rather not, we have a per-redemption-only plan too."

### "What if no customers come?"
> "Then you pay $0 in redemption costs — that's the whole point. With ad spend, you pay whether anyone walks in or not. With LYMX, you pay only when a real transaction happens. The $199 is your network access fee; the actual value transfer happens transaction-by-transaction."

### "I'm already doing my own loyalty program."
> "Most businesses we sign are. LYMX runs alongside — your existing punch card / Square loyalty stays. The difference is LYMX brings customers from *other businesses* in the network, not just your existing regulars. Think of it as a second channel that costs you nothing until it works."

### "Can I see another business that's using it?"
> "Yep. We have [name a Founding Business — Melongs currently — show them the live listing on /browse]. I can introduce you to the owner. They signed up [N weeks/months] ago and can tell you exactly what their experience has been."

## Reminders for the partner

- **Don't oversell.** The pitch is unusual enough that overhyping it backfires. Let the numbers + the "we eat the cost, not you" line do the work.
- **Don't promise traffic.** Promise the *deal* (we pay you on redemption). Traffic depends on the network filling up; you can't guarantee it on day one.
- **Show, don't tell.** Open getlymx.com/business on your phone and let them see the live counter ticking up over your shoulder. That's more convincing than any deck.
- **Earn $750.** Every Business you sign earns YOU $750 + 11% recurring on their monthly fee. The deal scales for both sides.
