Hero headline A/B

The Home page hero headline rotates across an inventory every 10 days. This shows how each variant performed — views, and the share who then searched or clicked a conversion link. Higher rates = better headline.

Variants are defined in the Home page hero script (the HEADLINES array). Add a new line there and it auto-joins the rotation. "Search rate" = searches ÷ views; "CTA rate" = conversion-link clicks ÷ views. Give each variant a full 10-day window before judging.
