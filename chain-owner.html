FAQ

How big is "chain" considered?

Anything 2+ locations qualifies for chain-owner features. Most of our chains are 2-15 locations (independent owner-operators). 25+ location operations get bespoke treatment via the white-label tier.

Can I assign different LYMX rates per location?

Yes. Each location can have its own LYMX rate (5/$1, 3/$1, etc.). Useful if your downtown location has different margins than your suburban one.

Can I add or remove locations after sign-up?

Yes. Add a location: $50/mo + $0 incremental sign-up (already paid). Remove a location: 90-day wind-down for that location only; rest of chain unaffected.

What happens if I sell one location?

Asset transfer — the new owner takes over LYMX for that location only. Your chain account keeps the remaining locations. See business agreement section 10b.

Does each location need its own POS integration?

Yes — each location's POS is integrated separately because each handles its own transactions. The unified dashboard pulls them all together.

What about franchises — owners vs. franchisor?

If you're the franchisor (you grant franchises), each franchisee signs up independently. If you're a franchisee with multiple owned units (e.g., 3 Subway franchises you personally own), the chain features apply to you.
