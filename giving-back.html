Donate your LYMX

Customers can donate any portion of their LYMX balance directly to a vetted local nonprofit, right from their wallet. We match customer donations 1-to-1 up to $25k per quarter.

  • 1 LYMX = $0.01, donated at full redemption value
  • LYMX matches every customer donation 1:1, up to $25k/quarter
  • Choose from 14 vetted Las Vegas nonprofits
  • Receive a tax-deductible receipt by email
  • 100% of donated LYMX goes to the nonprofit (no fees)
