Common questions

How does the recipient redeem LYMX?
Recipients get a personalized email with their balance. They visit any LYMX-accepting Business, show the QR code from their wallet, and pay. They can also use it across multiple visits — no “use it all at once” constraint.
What if my recipient already has a LYMX account?
Even better — we add the gift balance directly to their existing wallet. Their existing earned LYMX, gifts, and promotional balance live side by side.
Do unused LYMX expire?
No — issued LYMX has no expiration. The recipient can spend it whenever they want, across as many participating Businesses as they want.
Can recipients use it in multiple cities?
LYMX is launching in Las Vegas Q3 2026. As we expand to new cities (San Diego, Phoenix, Denver, Austin), the gift balance follows. Recipients can spend it in any city LYMX is live in.
Can I customize the gift card design?
Yes — Tier 2+ orders include custom branded card design (your logo, colors, message). Send us your brand assets, we'll have a proof for you in 2 business days.
How do I pay?
After you submit your order, we email you an invoice. Pay by ACH, wire, or credit card. Net-30 terms available for Tier 3 + Tier 4 orders.
What if a recipient never redeems their gift?
Per our terms, unused LYMX remains spendable indefinitely. Once delivered, the LYMX is the recipient's. We don't refund unused balance back to the bulk buyer (the gift was given). For corporate accounting, treat each delivered gift as a marketing/HR expense at its face value.
Can I track who has redeemed?
Yes — your dashboard shows aggregate redemption stats (% redeemed, average days-to-first-redemption, top Businesses redeemed at). To respect recipient privacy, we don't expose individual redemption activity to bulk buyers.
Is this a tax-deductible business expense?
Generally yes for employee gifts and customer-appreciation programs, subject to IRS limits. Talk to your accountant. We provide an itemized invoice for your records.
Do you offer monthly subscriptions for ongoing programs?
Yes — for “Friday lunch”-style recurring programs we set up auto-distribution: each Friday morning, every employee on your list gets their LYMX. We bill monthly. Talk to sales.
