Three generations of downline

When you sponsor another Partner who then activates their own Businesses, you earn a small override on their results too — three generations deep.

G1 — Direct

Partners you personally sponsored. You earn override on every Business they activate.

G2 — Their sponsorees

Partners that your G1 sponsored. You earn a smaller override on their activations.

G3 — Two layers deep

Partners that your G2 sponsored. Smallest override, but it adds up at scale.

What does NOT count as a generation: your own Businesses. Businesses you personally activate are Production, not a generation. Only downline Partners create G1/G2/G3. This is why the Commission Calculator separates "Own Businesses" from "Downline".

How to start earning

1

Use your partner code on every share

Every business owner you talk to should land at getlymx.com/biz-signup.html?ref=YOUR-CODE. Your code attributes the activation to you. Get your link in the Refer tab.

2

Send your prospects to the right page

Use biz-signup for businesses, welcome for customers, partner-signup for other Partners. The link you share has to match the role they're signing up as — otherwise the attribution chain breaks.

3

Watch your activations land

Open the Refer page and the Rep dashboard to see who signed up under you. A Business activation pays out the moment they complete the 20-minute onboarding call with Rachel and turn LYMX on at their counter — typically within 5 business days of signup.

4

Get paid

Activation bonuses + monthly recurring are paid out via Stripe Connect. Set your payout method on the Payouts page. Payouts run on the 1st of every month for everything earned the prior month.
