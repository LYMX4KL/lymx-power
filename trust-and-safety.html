How we prevent fraud

Every LYMX transaction passes through a real-time fraud detection layer. We use a mix of pattern matching, machine learning, and human review for edge cases. Here's what that looks like in practice:

99.96%

Of transactions clear automatically

The vast majority of LYMX activity looks normal — customers earning at one Business, redeeming at another. These pass without any friction.

0.04%

Are flagged for human review

Patterns like unusually high redemptions in a short window, sign-up surges from one IP, or device fingerprints we've seen before. A real person reviews each flag within 4 hours during business days.

< 0.01%

Result in a frozen wallet or account

If we determine fraud occurred, we may freeze the wallet pending investigation. Owners receive an email with details and a clear appeals path.

How we enforce our policies

If a Business, Partner, or customer breaks our terms, here's our process. We try to be fair and consistent.

Suspected fraudulent activity

Step 1 — Auto-flag. Suspicious activity triggers an alert. The wallet may be frozen pending investigation if the pattern is severe.

Step 2 — Human review. A Trust & Safety team member reviews the flag within 4 hours (business days). If it's a false positive, wallet unfreezes immediately.

Step 3 — User notification. If the freeze stands, the wallet owner receives an email with what we found, what they can do, and how to appeal.

Step 4 — Appeal. Reply to the email or contact support. A different team member reviews. Decision within 5 business days.

Terms-of-service violations

Examples: a Business misrepresenting LYMX redemption rules, a Partner making earnings claims that violate FTC rules, a customer attempting to game referral bonuses.

First violation: Warning + education. We point you to the relevant terms section and ask you to fix the issue.

Second violation: 30-day suspension of the relevant feature. Partners cannot recruit during this period; Businesses cannot run promos; customers cannot redeem.

Third violation: Account closure. Account-closure decisions are reviewed by senior staff.

Spam & harassment from Partners

Partners are independent contractors but represent the LYMX network when they prospect. Behaviors we don't tolerate:

  • Cold-emailing prospect lists you don't own (CAN-SPAM violations)
  • Making earnings claims that exceed FTC guidance
  • Misrepresenting LYMX or LYMX features in pitches
  • High-pressure sales tactics, especially with vulnerable Businesses

Partners caught engaging in these have their referral commission paused pending investigation. Repeat offenders are removed from the program.

Malicious or fake reviews

Customer reviews on Business profiles are moderated. We remove:

  • Reviews that contain personal attacks, threats, or hate speech
  • Reviews from accounts with no transaction history at the Business
  • Reviews flagged by Businesses as defamatory (subject to our review)
  • Reviews that appear to be coordinated (multiple from one IP, identical text)

We do not remove negative reviews just because they're negative. A 1-star review from a real customer with a real complaint stays up.

Stolen or lost wallet

If a customer reports their wallet stolen or accessed by someone else, we freeze the wallet immediately and contact them via verified phone number.

Once identity is verified, we transfer the LYMX balance to a new wallet (reset on a new device). The old wallet is permanently disabled.

Lost LYMX is recoverable in 100% of cases when reported within 30 days of the loss event. Outside that window, recovery depends on case specifics.
