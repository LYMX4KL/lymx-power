How the math works

+

Network referral revenue

Customers from other LYMX Businesses redeeming at yours. Calculated as: (daily traffic × ticket × referral lift × open days). They pay full ticket; you net new revenue.

+$1,620
+

Retention lift revenue

Existing customers come back more often. Calculated as: (current monthly revenue × retention lift × adoption). Compounds month over month.

+$1,008
−

Settlement cost (when LYMX is redeemed at another Business)

You pay only 80% of face value at settlement when LYMX you issued is redeemed at another Business — the 20% gap is the network's automatic referral discount. Plus a 3% processing fee in LYMX. Calculator assumes 5 LYMX/$1 issuance rate (the most common choice), but you set your own rate at activation.

−$418
−

Monthly subscription

Fixed $199/mo after the 3-month free trial included with your $850 sign-up. Multi-location adds $50/mo per extra location.
