How LYMX handles bank info

LYMX uses Stripe Connect to pay Partners. Your bank account is held by Stripe, not by LYMX — we never see your full routing or account number, and you can update it any time through Stripe's hosted form.

  • Account verification happens through Stripe (typically 1–2 micro-deposits).
  • Bank changes take effect immediately for future payouts — pending payouts stay on the previous bank until cleared.
  • Tax info (W-9 / 1099-NEC) is also collected through Stripe Connect.
