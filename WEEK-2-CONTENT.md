## 2. Chamber breakfast #1 — Tuesday May 19, 7:00 am PT

**What:** Show up. Network. Pitch only if invited; otherwise build relationships.

### Pre-event prep (Mon evening)

- [ ] Confirm event time + location with chamber
- [ ] Bring 50 printed business cards
- [ ] Bring 10 printed Founding-25 PDFs (folded in a leather portfolio, not loose)
- [ ] Wear something Vegas-business-appropriate (collared shirt, no jeans)
- [ ] Charge phone fully — you'll be taking contacts
- [ ] Set up Calendly QR code on your phone wallpaper

### At the event

**Don't pitch.** Networking events are about building reputation. Three things to do:

1. **Open conversations with curiosity.** "What kind of business are you in? How long have you been at it?" Listen for 3–5 minutes before mentioning yourself.
2. **When you mention LYMX:** keep it 30 seconds. "I'm building a loyalty rewards platform for Vegas small businesses. I'm spending the next month finding 25 Partners — sales-native locals who'd help bring businesses on. Would you know anyone?"
3. **Always exchange contact info.** Phone or LinkedIn. Add them to the prospect spreadsheet within 24 hours.

### Post-event (Tue evening)

- [ ] Add every name from the event to your prospect spreadsheet
- [ ] Send a personalized follow-up text to anyone who showed real interest within 24 hours: "Great meeting you this morning at the chamber. Here's the 1-pager I mentioned: lymx.netlify.app/Founding-25-Invitation.pdf. Coffee next week?"

### If you got a 5-min pitch slot

> "Good morning. I'm Kenny — I'm building LYMX Power, a loyalty rewards platform for small Vegas businesses.
>
> Quick thing about the chamber: every small business in this room has the same problem. Big chains know exactly who their repeat customers are. You don't. You serve them every week, you know their kids' names, but the data lives in your head — not your POS.
>
> LYMX fixes that. Three months free. No upfront cost. Each business that joins brings about 1,500 of their own customers into the local LYMX network monthly. Customer data finally lives somewhere you can see it.
>
> I'm looking for 25 Partners — Vegas-based, sales-native, networked — to bring businesses on board. Permanent founding perks for the first 25 to qualify. $750 cash per activation, plus 11% recurring on every subscription.
