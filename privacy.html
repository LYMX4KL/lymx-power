Privacy Policy

Effective date: TBD upon launch · Last updated: April 28, 2026 · Governing law: Nevada

This Privacy Policy explains what information LYMX (operated by LYMX Network, "LYMX", "we", "us") collects from you, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. We try to write it in plain English. If anything is unclear, email us — see contact at the bottom.

Short version. We collect the minimum we need to run the LYMX network: your phone, name, transactions, and basic device info. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. We share it with participating Businesses only as needed to process LYMX transactions. You can download or delete your data anytime from your account settings.

1. Information we collect

From customers

  • Account info: phone number (required to verify your account), name, email (optional), password (stored hashed)
  • Transaction info: when you earn or spend LYMX — which Business, when, how much LYMX, and an itemized receipt if the Business provides one
  • Device info: browser type, operating system, IP address, approximate location (city-level only, used to show nearby Businesses)
  • Optional content: reviews, photos, profile preferences if you submit them

From Businesses

  • Business name, address, contact info, owner identity, payment account (ACH details for settlement)
  • Issuance and redemption activity at your store
  • Listing content (description, photos, hours)

From Partners (independent contractors)

  • Contact info, address, sponsor relationship, W-9 details, ACH bank account for commission payouts
  • Activity in the Partner CRM and dashboard

2. How we use it

  • To run your account, process LYMX issuance and redemption, calculate settlement, pay Partner commissions
  • To send transactional notifications (earn/redemption confirmations, settlement statements, security alerts)
  • To improve the product — anonymized usage patterns help us decide what to build next
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax forms, regulator requests where required)

3. Who we share it with

  • Participating Businesses — when you earn or spend LYMX at their store, they see the transaction details we share with them (the LYMX amount, the time, your name on receipts if you've added one). They do not see your full balance, your activity at other Businesses, or your contact info beyond what's needed to process the transaction.
  • Service providers — vendors we use to operate (hosting, email delivery, payment processing, customer support). They handle data only as needed to perform the service for us, under written agreements.
  • Legal & safety — when required by law (subpoena, court order) or to protect against fraud, security threats, or harm.
  • Business transfers — if LYMX is acquired or restructured, your data may transfer with the business. We will notify you in advance if that happens.

4. We do not sell your data

LYMX does not sell, rent, or license your personal information to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for their own marketing purposes. We don't run ads. We make money from Business subscriptions, sign-up fees, and the buy/sell mechanic on LYMX units — never from monetizing customer attention.

5. Cookies & similar tracking

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies — keep you logged in, remember your wallet preferences, prevent abuse
  • Analytics — privacy-respecting aggregate stats to understand which pages get used (no cross-site tracking)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or social media trackers.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Active account: as long as your account is open
  • Transaction records: 7 years (US tax / financial-recordkeeping requirements)
  • Reviews: until you delete them or your account, whichever first
  • Closed accounts: personal info deleted within 90 days unless we're required to retain transaction records as above

7. Your rights & controls

From your account settings (customer-settings.html) you can:

  • Update your name, email, phone, password
  • Toggle notifications (push, SMS, email)
  • Toggle public review visibility
  • Toggle location use
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Sign out
  • Delete your account

If you live in California, Nevada, the European Union, or other jurisdictions with applicable privacy law, you may have additional rights including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information, and the right to know what we collect. Email privacy@getlymx.com to exercise any of these rights — we'll respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard practices: HTTPS everywhere, hashed passwords, encrypted-at-rest databases, multi-factor authentication for staff. No system is bulletproof. If we ever discover a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you and the relevant authorities within the timeframe required by Nevada law and any other applicable regulations.

9. Children

LYMX is for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has created an account, email privacy@getlymx.com and we will remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in your wallet and via email at least 30 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.
