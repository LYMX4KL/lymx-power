FAQ

How does the 1:1 match work?

You donate LYMX (which has redemption value at participating Businesses). LYMX simultaneously donates an equal cash amount directly to the nonprofit. Your $10 of LYMX → $10 of LYMX redeemable by the nonprofit + $10 in cash from LYMX = $20 total impact.

What does the nonprofit do with the LYMX?

They redeem at participating Businesses for goods + services. Some buy meals from LYMX restaurants for their programs. Some use it for office supplies, supplies for events, etc. They have full flexibility.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes — for the cash-equivalent value of donated LYMX. The nonprofit issues you a tax receipt. Consult your tax advisor for specifics. Note: only the LYMX value (your donation) is tax-deductible to you; the LYMX 1:1 match is deductible by LYMX, not by you.

Can I suggest a nonprofit to add to the program?

Yes — email charity@lymx.com with the nonprofit's name + 501(c)(3) status + Vegas connection. We add 1-3 new partners per quarter based on customer + Business votes.

Is there a cap on the LYMX 1:1 match?

$25,000/quarter network-wide. If we hit the cap, donations still go through but the match pauses for the rest of the quarter. We notify everyone when match resets.

What about Businesses donating their LYMX revenue?

Businesses can donate LYMX too — see giving-back for the Business-side donation tools. They're separate from customer donations but contribute to the same nonprofits.

All nonprofits are verified 501(c)(3) entities with active Vegas operations. Annual audit at transparency report.
