How Daniel runs the place

Before LYMX

Brew & Bean opened in 2024 — independent, single location, 5 baristas, ~600 daily transactions. Owner Daniel Whitley had already tried 3 different "punch card" loyalty programs. Customers kept losing the cards. Cardless options needed customers to download yet another app.

"By the end of 2025 I'd given up on loyalty as a concept. People want coffee, they don't want a relationship with my POS system."

What flipped it

"What flipped it was the network angle. I'd been a customer of two other Launch 25 Businesses for months. The day my customer used LYMX they earned at Oakline Kitchen at my counter, the math made sense. They didn't want a relationship with my POS — they wanted a relationship with the neighborhood."

"I signed in week 4. My welcome promo doubles LYMX on first visits. About 30% of new customers I see now mention they tried me because of LYMX."

"Loyalty programs used to ask my customers to download apps for me. LYMX asks them to take part in something bigger than my coffee shop. That's a different conversation."
Daniel Whitley · Owner, Brew & Bean Coffee · April 2026 Spotlight
