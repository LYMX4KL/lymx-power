How we calculated these numbers

We're committed to transparent reporting. Here's the methodology behind the figures above.

  • Customer spending kept local: Total LYMX-tracked customer spending at small Businesses Feb 14 – Apr 30, multiplied by the difference in local-recirculation rate ($0.71 vs $0.32 = $0.39 differential).
  • Jobs supported: Median # of employees per Business category × number of Businesses in that category. Excludes part-time-equivalent reduction.
  • Local sales taxes: 8.375% Las Vegas sales tax × LYMX-tracked taxable sales Q1 2026.
  • Nonprofit donations: Direct customer donate-LYMX + LYMX 1:1 match + Founding 25 community grants. YTD as of Apr 30, 2026.

All figures audited internally. Independent third-party audit planned for FY 2026 annual report. See transparency report for additional methodology.
