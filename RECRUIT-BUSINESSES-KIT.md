- Customer's phone vibrates with the credit confirmation
- Customer can spend their LYMX at ANY OTHER LYMX Business — including yours, on a future visit

## 6. Objection handlers

> "I already use Square Loyalty / Clover punch cards / [other loyalty]."

You can run them in parallel. LYMX doesn't replace your existing program — it gives your customers a SECOND reason to come in. The big difference is that LYMX customers are already earning rewards at OTHER local Businesses, so when they walk past you, they have a balance burning a hole in their pocket. That's a reason to enter that didn't exist before. Best-of-both-worlds: keep your existing program for "regulars," let LYMX bring in "network walk-ins."

> "I don't want to give discounts."

LYMX isn't discounts. It's a credit you ISSUE to the customer at a rate of YOUR choosing (default 5%, you can set it lower). The customer ALWAYS pays full price at your register. You only "lose" anything if/when they later REDEEM their LYMX at YOUR Business — and even then, redemption is capped at 80% of any single transaction (the 80% rule). For most Businesses, redemptions are a fraction of issuances, so the net is positive — you're getting customer-acquisition data and traffic in exchange for an effective ~3% off when LYMX customers come back.

> "$199/mo is more than my POS subscription."

Compare to: a single Yelp ad spot is $300-$1,000/mo for visibility. A SEO consultant is $1,500/mo. A loyalty platform like Belly or FiveStars is $200-$300/mo. LYMX is the same price as a loyalty platform but you get the network on top — your existing customers earn rewards at OTHER Businesses (which they bring you "value-added" instead of "spent here only"), and OTHER Businesses' customers walk into yours. It's loyalty + acquisition stacked.

> "What if I cancel?"

Standard month-to-month after the 3-month trial. No long-term contract. You can pause or cancel any time from your Business dashboard.

> "How do I know LYMX won't disappear in 6 months?"

Fair. Two answers: (1) Founding 25 deals lock in pricing forever, so even if we 10× the monthly later, you stay at $199. (2) The platform is built on Supabase — even if LYMX-the-company changes shape, your customer data is exportable and your Business records are yours. We treat your data like an InvestPro Realty rent-roll: you own it.

> "Will my staff hate this?"

The cashier flow is: scan customer phone (1 second), nothing else. No new buttons, no decisions, no upsell. Most Businesses run it for a week and forget it's there.
