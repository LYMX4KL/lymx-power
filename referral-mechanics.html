Frequently asked questions

Is there a cap on how many friends I can refer?

No cap on direct referrals. Some active customers have referred 50+. The platform doesn't penalize you for referring a lot.

What if my friend signs up but never transacts?

The bonus doesn't unlock until they make their first transaction. We do this to prevent abuse. Fully fair to you — if they never use it, no bonus, but also no harm done.

Can I refer family or only people outside my household?

Family is fine. Refer your spouse, your kids, your parents — as long as each has a real phone number and makes real transactions, the bonus is paid.

What if my friend already has a LYMX wallet?

Then the referral doesn't apply — they're already a customer. The bonus is for first-time customers only.

Can I take back a referral if my friend gets weird about it?

You don't need to. Your bonus is yours; theirs is theirs. The two are independent once they're paid.

What about Business referrals (instead of customer referrals)?

That's a different program — LYMX Partner program. Earn $500 + ongoing commission for referring a Business to LYMX. Different from customer referrals (which is what this page is about). Learn about Partners →

How do I see my referrals?

Open your wallet → Refer page. See pending referrals (signed up but no first transaction yet) + earned referrals (already paid). Real-time.
