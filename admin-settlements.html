Business Settlements

Monthly clearing-house settlement queue. Run a settlement batch to compute pending rows for a period; approve and pay each row individually. ACH cadence: 5th business day of each month, covering the previous calendar month.

Default period: previous calendar month. Re-running the same period is idempotent — existing settlement rows are returned unchanged.
