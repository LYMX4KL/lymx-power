How tier progression works

Tiers are forward-looking — once you hit a tier, you stay there as long as your numbers stay above the threshold. If you drop below (e.g., a Direct Business cancels and you fall from 11 to 9 active), you stay at Gold for a 90-day grace period to recover. After that, you move down a tier. You're never permanently demoted — re-earn the next tier any time.

Founding 25 vs. certification tiers

These are orthogonal tracks. Founding 25 is an honorific for the first 25 Partners to hit 5 Direct activations — earned in launch phase. The certification ladder (Bronze → Master) is the ongoing tier system. A Master Partner can also be Founding 25 (and is a stronger Partner for it). They overlap but aren't the same thing.

FAQ

Do I lose my tier when a Business cancels?

Not immediately. 90-day grace period before tier demotion. Most Businesses renew during that window. If you stay below threshold for 90+ days, tier moves down — but you can re-earn anytime.

How is "Direct Business" counted?

A Business YOU personally signed (not via your downline). They appear in your "Production" tree, not in G1/G2/G3.

Can I be Master Partner AND Founding 25?

Yes — and it's the strongest combination. About 4 of the 25 Founding Partners have already hit Master tier. The honorifics stack.

What if I miss the Master threshold by 1?

You stay at Gold until you hit 25. We don't grade on a curve. The threshold is the threshold. Most Partners who get close push through within the next 30 days.

Is the 90-day grace period only for demotions?

Yes. Promotions are immediate when you hit threshold. Demotions wait 90 days to give recovery time.

Can I skip a tier?

No — tiers are sequential. You earn each on the way up. (E.g., if you signed 25 Businesses immediately, you'd hit Bronze → Silver → Gold → Master in days, but progression is still through each tier.)
