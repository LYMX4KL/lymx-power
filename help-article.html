How to redeem LYMX at a Business

Spending LYMX is the same flow at every Business in the network: open your wallet, tap Pay, show the QR code, the Business scans, done. Here's the full walk-through.

What you'll need

  • Your LYMX Web Wallet open on your phone (or installed as a Web App on your home screen)
  • An active LYMX balance — even 100 LYMX (the welcome bonus) is enough to start
  • You're at a participating LYMX Business — look for the LYMX QR signage at the counter, or check the directory

The 5 steps

1

Open your wallet on your phone

Tap the LYMX icon on your home screen. If you haven't installed it as a Web App yet, just open getlymx.com/wallet in your phone browser.

2

Tap "Pay with QR" on the balance card

It's the gold button on the blue balance card. A full-screen QR code appears.

3

Tell the cashier "I'd like to use my LYMX balance"

The cashier opens their LYMX POS app and selects "Accept LYMX" — they enter the dollar amount you want to apply, scan your QR, and the LYMX equivalent is deducted from your balance.

4

Approve the amount on your phone (if it's over your auto-approve limit)

By default, redemptions over $20 require a quick tap-to-approve on your phone. You can change this limit in Settings.

5

Done — you'll see a receipt notification

Both you and the Business get a confirmation. The receipt shows up in your inbox within seconds, and you can find it again later in your activity history.

How the math works

Each LYMX = $0.01 of spending power at any participating Business. Some quick examples:

  • 1,000 LYMX = $10 of value applied to your bill
  • 5,000 LYMX = $50 of value
  • 10,000 LYMX = $100 of value

If your bill is $14.50 and you have 2,000 LYMX, you can apply your full $20 of LYMX value (which covers your $14.50 bill — the remaining $5.50 of value stays in your wallet) or apply just $14.50 worth (1,450 LYMX) and keep 550 LYMX for next time. More on partial redemption below.

Earning vs. spending — they're the same balance

If a Business gives you 2× LYMX during their Happy Hour multiplier, those new LYMX go straight into the same wallet. There's no "earn balance" and "spend balance" — just one number.

Partial redemption

You don't have to spend all your LYMX at once. The cashier asks you, "How much LYMX would you like to apply?" and you can pick any amount up to your balance.

Common patterns:

  1. Full coverage: apply enough LYMX to cover your whole bill. Pay $0 in cash.
  2. Partial coverage: apply some LYMX, pay the rest in cash or card. Helpful when you're saving up for a big redemption later.
  3. Top-off only: apply just enough to round your bill to a nice number. Some customers find this satisfying.

If something goes wrong

QR scanner won't read my code

The QR refreshes every 90 seconds for security. If the cashier can't scan, tap the Refresh button on the QR screen and try again. If it still doesn't work, brighten your phone screen — most issues are dim displays under bright counter lighting.

The deducted amount doesn't match what the cashier entered

Stop the transaction immediately. Tap Dispute on the receipt screen — it opens a support ticket and freezes the transaction until our team reviews it. Both you and the Business owner get an email with next steps.

Never give your QR code to anyone outside the Business

Your QR is a one-time-use redemption ticket. Don't text it, email it, or post it online. The Business scans it directly from your phone screen — that's the only way to use it.

I don't have enough LYMX to cover my bill

That's fine — apply what you have. Pay the difference with cash or card as you normally would. The Business POS handles split payment automatically.

The Business says they don't accept LYMX

Sometimes a Business is listed but their POS isn't activated yet. Check the LYMX QR at the counter — if there's no signage, ask the manager. If they say they're not on LYMX, find a different LYMX Business nearby. We're growing the network fast.

Quick FAQ

Do my LYMX expire?

No. LYMX has no expiration. You can earn them today and spend them next year.

Can I transfer LYMX to a friend?

Not directly, but you can refer them — both of you get 200 LYMX when they sign up. It's the simplest way to "share."

Can I get cash for my LYMX?

No. LYMX is a promotional credit, not money. It's spendable across LYMX Businesses, but not redeemable for cash. More about how LYMX works.

Do I need a separate app?

No native app — LYMX runs as a Web App from your browser. Add it to your home screen via these instructions for the smoothest experience.
