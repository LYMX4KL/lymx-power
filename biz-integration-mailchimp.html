How to connect Mailchimp

One-time setup. ⏱️ Total: ~12 min

1

Open Integrations in your LYMX dashboard

From your Business dashboard, click Settings → Integrations. Find Mailchimp and click Connect.

~30 sec
2

Authorize via Mailchimp OAuth

Sign in to your Mailchimp account. Authorize LYMX. Permissions: read/write to one audience (you pick which).

~2 min
3

Pick which Mailchimp audience to sync

If you have multiple audiences in Mailchimp, pick one. Most Businesses have one main audience for their customers. Custom: you can create a new "LYMX Customers" audience just for this sync.

~1 min
4

Map LYMX merge fields to Mailchimp

Map LYMX-specific fields (balance, last visit, total spend, top category) to Mailchimp merge tags. Defaults work for 90% of cases — review and confirm.

~3 min
5

Set up automation triggers

Choose which automated emails to enable: welcome series, winback, birthday, anniversary. Each comes with a starter template you can customize.

~5 min
6

Run a test sync

Click "Sync Now" — your LYMX contacts pull into Mailchimp. Verify they show up with correct tags + merge fields. Done.

~2 min

Frequently asked questions

What about Klaviyo, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, etc.?

Klaviyo integration is available (similar setup). ConvertKit + ActiveCampaign are on the roadmap (Q3 2026). For now, those work via CSV export.

Will customers who opt out of LYMX marketing also opt out of my Mailchimp?

Yes. We respect the unsubscribe in either direction. If a customer hits "unsubscribe" in Mailchimp, their LYMX marketing-opt-in goes off. If they opt out in LYMX, they're removed from your Mailchimp sync.

What if I run my own emails outside Mailchimp?

You can also build a Mailchimp campaign without LYMX-triggered automation. The integration still gives you better segmentation. Or skip Mailchimp entirely — use the LYMX dashboard's built-in email tools.

How does this affect my Mailchimp pricing?

Mailchimp prices by audience size. The LYMX integration adds your LYMX customers to your audience, which may increase your Mailchimp tier. We recommend creating a separate "LYMX Customers" audience to control this.

Can I sync only a subset of customers?

Yes. In setup step 3, you can apply a filter (e.g., "only sync customers with at least 1 transaction"). This keeps your Mailchimp list focused.

What about GDPR / CAN-SPAM compliance?

We sync only customers who have opted in to marketing emails through their LYMX wallet (explicit consent). The opt-in is stored as a merge field. CAN-SPAM compliant by design.
