Step 1Install the LYMX POS app on your iPad

⏱ 10 min

The LYMX POS is a Web App — there's nothing to download from the App Store.

  1. Plug the iPad into power. Open Safari (not Chrome — Safari has better support for the home-screen install we'll do).
  2. Go to getlymx.com/pos.
  3. Sign in with the email you used to activate. Check your email if you didn't pick a password — we sent a magic link.
  4. Tap the Share icon at the top of Safari → Add to Home Screen. Name it "LYMX POS".
  5. Close Safari. Open "LYMX POS" from your home screen. This is the URL your staff will use every day.
💡
Tip: in iOS Settings → Display & Brightness, set Auto-Lock to Never while the iPad is plugged in. The POS doesn't time out, but the iPad's screen will sleep otherwise.

Step 2Pair the thermal printer

⏱ 10 min

If you're not printing physical receipts (most cafes don't), skip this step. The POS works fine without a printer — receipts default to email + SMS.

  1. Plug the printer into power. Plug a network cable from your router into the printer (preferred) or turn on the printer's Bluetooth pairing mode (hold the Feed button for 5s).
  2. On the iPad, open the LYMX POS → Settings → Printer.
  3. Tap Discover printers. Your Star TSP100 should appear within 30 seconds.
  4. Tap it to pair. The POS will print a test receipt that says "PAIRED". Tape that test receipt to the wall behind the printer — you'll thank yourself in 6 months when troubleshooting.
  5. Print a sample receipt with Preview receipt template → tap Print. Adjust the paper-cut setting if the receipt is shorter or longer than expected.
⚠️
If pairing fails: the most common cause is a Bluetooth conflict with another device. Forget all other Bluetooth devices on the iPad and try again. If still stuck, text Sam at 702-555-LYMX.

Step 3Place the QR signage

⏱ 5 min

The customer-facing QR signs invite customers to sign up for a LYMX wallet at the moment they're paying. They drive most of your customer acquisition in week 1.

Where to put each piece:

  • Counter sign (large) — next to the POS, visible from where the customer pays. Their eyes will land on it while they wait.
  • Counter sign (small) — by the cash register or tip jar.
  • 4 table tents — one per table at restaurants. For cafes/retail, scatter near the entrance + at the pickup counter.
💡
Customize the welcome message: on the back of the kit you'll find QR stickers with your Business name pre-printed. Peel and stick them to the table tents to localize.

Step 4Test a real transaction with your phone

⏱ 10 min

Before you let real customers near the system, run through one issue + one redemption with your own phone.

  1. Sign yourself up at getlymx.com/join using the QR sign on your counter. Get the 100-LYMX welcome bonus.
  2. On the POS iPad, tap Issue LYMX. Enter your phone number. Enter a $10 ticket subtotal. Tap Issue.
  3. Check your phone — your wallet should now show your welcome bonus + the 50 LYMX (5/$1 base) you just earned.
  4. On the iPad, tap Accept LYMX. Enter your phone number again. Enter $1.50 to redeem (= 150 LYMX from your balance).
  5. Tap Show QR on your phone. Scan it with the iPad camera. Confirm.
  6. Print the receipt (if you set up the printer in step 2).

Step 5Train your staff (15-min video + 5-min Q&A)

⏱ 25 min · whole-staff session

This is the highest-ROI 25 minutes of the whole setup. Trained staff convert customers; untrained staff create friction.

  1. Gather your full counter staff. Even part-timers.
  2. Play the staff training video on the POS iPad — 15 minutes total. Covers: what to say to first-time customers, how to issue, how to accept, what to do if something goes wrong.
  3. Walk one staff member through a real transaction at the counter, like step 4. Have everyone watch.
  4. Open Q&A. Most common questions:
    • "What if a customer's QR code expires?" → Tap Refresh on their phone.
    • "What if their phone is dead?" → Take their phone number manually, they earn the LYMX retroactively.
    • "Can I edit a transaction after it's posted?" → Not for the first 60 seconds. After that, file a settlement dispute.
  5. Make sure every staff member knows the "first-time customer 2× promo" is active by default. They should mention it.
💡
The 30-second pitch your staff should know: "It's a shared loyalty program for local Las Vegas Businesses. You earn LYMX here, you can spend it at any other LYMX Business. No app to download. Want me to set you up while I ring this up?"

Step 6You're live. Now what.

Two things to do in your first week:

  1. Schedule a Happy-Hour multiplier. Pick a slow window (e.g., 3-5 PM Tuesdays) and double the LYMX. Use promotions.html. Customers will start coming for it.
  2. Email your existing customer list. Use the email template in the content hub. Subject line: "We just joined LYMX — your first visit is on us." Plug your Business referral code so they get 100 LYMX welcome.
