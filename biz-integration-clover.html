How to connect Clover

Step-by-step setup. ⏱️ Total: ~15 min

1

Install LYMX from the Clover App Market

On your Clover Web Dashboard, go to Apps → App Market → search "LYMX". Click Install. The app deploys to all your Clover devices automatically.

~2 min
2

Open LYMX on the Clover device

A LYMX icon appears on your Clover home screen. Tap it. The first time, you'll be prompted to sign in to your LYMX account.

~1 min
3

Authorize the integration

Clover will ask permission for LYMX to read inventory and write transactions. Approve. This happens once per merchant account.

~1 min
4

Map inventory categories to LYMX multipliers

In the LYMX dashboard, walk through your Clover inventory categories. Confirm or set multipliers (default 1×). For specific items, you can boost individually.

~5 min
5

Test with a $1 sale

Ring up a test transaction on the Clover. Confirm the LYMX issuance appears on the receipt and in your LYMX dashboard. Refund the test sale to confirm reversal.

~2 min
6

Train staff (5 min)

New checkout flow: ask the customer "do you have LYMX?" → if yes, scan their QR. The Clover screen prompts the LYMX flow automatically when a LYMX customer is detected. Watch the cashier training video.

~4 min

Frequently asked questions

Does LYMX work with Clover's bundled loyalty app (Clover Loyalty)?

Yes. They run side-by-side without conflict. Most LYMX Businesses on Clover turn off Clover Loyalty after 30–60 days because LYMX adds the network referral mechanic that Clover Loyalty can't.

Can I use the Clover printer for LYMX receipts?

Yes. LYMX issuance and redemption appear as line items on the standard Clover receipt — printed automatically. No separate LYMX receipt needed.

What if my Clover device loses internet?

Clover transactions process offline (Clover handles this). LYMX issuances queue locally and replay when the connection returns. Customer-facing wallet operations are unaffected — they don't depend on your Clover.

Multiple Clover merchants — can I roll them up?

Yes. If you operate multiple merchant accounts under one ownership, LYMX rolls them up to a single LYMX Business profile (or splits them into separate profiles, your choice). Configure in Settings → Multi-merchant.

Does LYMX charge anything per transaction?

No transaction fee from LYMX. The only LYMX cost is the wholesale rate ($0.008 per LYMX issued) + your monthly subscription. Clover's standard transaction fees apply separately.

What if I switch to a different POS later?

Your LYMX data (customers, transaction history, settings) lives in LYMX, not Clover. If you switch POS later, you can disconnect Clover and connect a different POS without losing data.
