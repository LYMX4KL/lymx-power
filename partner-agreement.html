Partner Independent Contractor Agreement

Effective when accepted by Partner at sign-up · Last updated: April 28, 2026 · Governing law: Nevada

This Partner Agreement (the "Agreement") is between LYMX Network ("LYMX", "we") and the individual who applies and is accepted to become a LYMX Partner ("Partner", "you"). This Agreement is incorporated by reference into the LYMX Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and LYMX Disclaimer.

Read carefully — independent contractor. You are not an employee of LYMX. You're not entitled to wages, benefits, payroll tax withholding, workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance. You're responsible for your own taxes. See section 2 for full details.

1. Independent contractor status

You and LYMX agree that you are entering into this Agreement as an independent contractor, not as an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of LYMX. You will not be treated as an employee for federal, state, or local tax purposes.

  • You set your own hours, your own pace, and your own methods of recruiting
  • You provide your own tools (phone, computer, transportation)
  • You are not entitled to wages, salary, employee benefits, payroll tax withholding, workers' compensation, unemployment insurance, or any other employee-related benefit
  • You are responsible for your own self-employment taxes (federal income tax, Social Security, Medicare, state taxes)
  • LYMX will issue a Form 1099-NEC each January reporting cash compensation paid to you in the prior calendar year if it exceeds the IRS reporting threshold
  • Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time per section 10

2. Your role & duties

As a Partner, your primary role is to recruit Businesses to the LYMX network. Specifically, you may:

  • Identify, prospect, and pitch local Businesses
  • Walk Business owners through the LYMX value proposition using approved materials
  • Provide your unique referral link/code to Business owners during sign-up
  • Continue to support activated Businesses (informally) and recruit additional Partners

You may NOT:

  • Bind LYMX to contracts, obligations, or representations
  • Represent yourself as a LYMX employee, officer, or agent
  • Make promises or guarantees on behalf of LYMX beyond what's stated in approved materials
  • Collect money on behalf of LYMX (Businesses pay LYMX directly)

3. No purchase requirement (anti-pyramid)

You are not required to purchase any product or pay any fee to participate as a Partner, beyond optional training or kit materials that LYMX may offer. There is no minimum activation quota, no inventory commitment, no product to stock.

Partner compensation is paid only on bona fide third-party Business sales — Business sign-up fees and ongoing subscriptions paid by Businesses to LYMX. No portion of your compensation is based on Partner recruitment alone or on Partner purchases.

This Agreement is structured to comply with the FTC Act, the Nevada Deceptive Trade Practices Act (NRS 598), the Nevada pyramid-scheme statute (NRS 207.235), and applicable state multi-level marketing laws.

5. $500 sign-up bonus mechanics

For each Direct Business you recruit that successfully activates a paid Enterprise plan, you receive a $500 cash sign-up bonus, paid in two installments:

  • $250 on day 7 after the Business's activation (subject to no chargeback or cancellation in that window)
  • $250 on day 30 after the Business's activation, contingent on the Business remaining active

The two-installment structure is anti-fraud insurance. If a Business signs up and immediately cancels or charges back, LYMX's exposure is limited.

Sign-up bonuses are direct-only. G1, G2, and G3 Partners do not receive a slice of the sign-up bonus on Businesses recruited by Partners deeper in the tree — only the Direct Partner receives this bonus.

6. LYMX-denominated commissions

The LYMX-denominated portion of your commission (column 4 in section 4) is credited to your network LYMX balance — not paid as cash. LYMX commissions:

  • Are spendable at any participating LYMX Business at face value (1 LYMX = $0.01)
  • Are not convertible to cash, ACH, gift card, money order, or any monetary instrument from LYMX
  • Are not transferable to other Partners or customers
  • Are not securities and confer no investment rights or returns
  • May be considered taxable income at the time of credit at face value (consult your tax advisor)

7. Payment terms (ACH, W-9, taxes)

Cash compensation: Sign-up bonuses and monthly cash overrides are paid via ACH on the 5th business day of each calendar month for activity in the prior month.

W-9 required: Before your first commission payout, you must provide a completed and signed IRS Form W-9 with a valid Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN).

Tax responsibility: You are solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes on income earned under this Agreement. LYMX does not withhold taxes from commission payments. LYMX will issue Form 1099-NEC each January for cash compensation (and possibly LYMX-denominated compensation, depending on counsel guidance) paid in the prior calendar year if it exceeds the IRS reporting threshold.

Bank account: You provide your ACH details (routing + account) at activation. You may update them at any time from your dashboard. If a payment fails (insufficient funds, wrong routing, closed account), we will reach out and reschedule.

8. Marketing & advertising rules

Federal and Nevada law impose strict rules on what you can say while recruiting Businesses or other Partners. You agree:

  • No earnings claims. You may not represent specific income amounts, percentages, or guarantees of any kind. Statements like "you'll make $X/month" or "you'll earn $X with N referrals" are prohibited even if mathematically correct, unless they are explicitly framed as illustrative projections matching LYMX-approved materials.
  • No false statements. All representations about LYMX must match approved Partner materials (the content hub, partner-signup, business-pitch pages).
  • No misrepresentation of LYMX units. You may not represent LYMX units as cash, securities, currency, or investments. Always describe LYMX as a "promotional credit unit redeemable at participating Businesses."
  • Lifestyle marketing prohibited. You may not depict yourself or other Partners with imagery suggesting wealth, success, or lifestyle outcomes attributable to LYMX participation.
  • FTC compliance. When recommending LYMX in social media or other public contexts, you must disclose your Partner relationship clearly (e.g., "I'm a LYMX Partner — I get paid if you sign up").

9. Prohibited conduct

  • Self-dealing or fictitious activations to trigger sign-up bonuses
  • Paying or rebating customers / Businesses to sign up under your link
  • Using stolen, scraped, or unsolicited contact lists for cold recruiting (CAN-SPAM, TCPA compliance is your responsibility)
  • Falsifying recruiter identity or pretending to be another Partner
  • Disparaging other Partners or Businesses publicly
  • Reselling or transferring your Partner account or downstream tree

10. Term, suspension & termination

This Agreement starts when LYMX accepts your Partner application and continues until terminated. Either party may terminate at any time, for any reason, by providing notice.

Suspension: LYMX may suspend your Partner account immediately for suspected fraud, violations of this Agreement, or violations of applicable law. Suspended accounts cannot earn commissions during suspension.

Termination consequences:

  • Earned but unpaid cash commissions are paid through the next regular payment cycle, except in cases of fraud where they may be forfeit.
  • LYMX-denominated commissions in your balance remain spendable at LYMX Businesses for 90 days after termination, then expire.
  • Your downstream Partner tree is reassigned to LYMX or to another active Partner at LYMX's discretion. The downstream Partners themselves are not affected — they keep their own accounts and tree.
  • You return any LYMX-issued materials (badges, credentials).

11. Liability & indemnification

To the maximum extent permitted by law, LYMX's total liability arising out of or relating to this Agreement is limited to the cash commissions paid to you in the 12 months preceding the event giving rise to liability.

You agree to indemnify and hold LYMX harmless from any claim, loss, or liability arising out of: your statements or representations (especially earnings claims), your tax obligations, your interactions with prospective Businesses or Partners, your violation of applicable law, or your violation of this Agreement.

12. General provisions

  • Governing law: Nevada, USA. Disputes resolved per the arbitration clause in section 11 of the Terms of Service.
  • Entire agreement: This Agreement, the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Disclaimer, is the entire agreement between you and LYMX regarding your role as a Partner.
  • Modification: LYMX may update this Agreement with at least 30 days' notice. Continued participation after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
  • Assignment: You may not assign this Agreement. LYMX may assign in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.
  • Severability: If any part is unenforceable, the rest remains in effect.
  • No earnings projections: Any earnings figures shown anywhere on LYMX surfaces, including the income projection calculator, are illustrative based on inputs you provide. They do not represent typical Partner earnings or guarantees of any kind. The majority of Partners may earn less than the figures shown — actual results depend entirely on your effort, your network, and external factors outside LYMX's control.
