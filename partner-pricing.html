FAQ

Why charge a Partner fee at all?

The $25 sign-up + $12.95/mo are filtering mechanisms, not revenue plays. We want Partners who are committed enough to put a small skin in the game. The fees barely cover dashboard hosting + support. Until 7/31/2027 they're waived to remove all friction during launch.

What if I sign up free now and become inactive — do fees retroactively apply?

No. Once you sign up during the free window, your sign-up is free permanently. Monthly fees only kick in 12 months later — and only if you're still actively using the dashboard.

Can I pay annually for the $100 to save money?

Yes — $100/year is cheaper than $12.95 × 12 = $155.40. Save 35% by paying annually. Available once paid fees activate (post-grace).

What if I cancel my Partner status?

You can cancel anytime. Already-earned commissions stay paid. Future MGC overrides freeze. If you re-activate within 6 months, status restored. After 6 months: re-sign as new Partner (subject to whatever fees are current).

Are Founding 25 Partners actually exempt forever?

Yes — provided they maintain their cooperative qualifying status (3 credits/year). See founding-partners-qualifying.html. If they fail qualifying for 3 consecutive years, they revert to standard fees going forward.

Does the $25 fee apply to spouses / business partners on my account?

One Partner login per Partner account. Spouses or business partners would each need their own Partner sign-up. Volume-discount for multi-Partner shops not available — each operates independently.
