🤖 AI & Data Use

How LYMX uses AI — and what we won't do with your data

Effective April 30, 2026 · Last updated April 30, 2026 · Read alongside our Privacy Policy

Our promise in 4 lines

✗
We do not train AI models on your data. Not your transactions, not your wallet history, not your support tickets — not by us, and not by the AI providers we use internally.
✗
We do not sell your data to AI companies, ad networks, or data brokers. We do not sell your data, period. (See Privacy Policy.)
✗
We do not use AI to make adverse decisions about you — no "AI denied your wallet" or "AI flagged you as risky" without a human reviewing the context.
✓
We do use AI for narrow, transparent jobs — fraud detection, support routing, content drafts that staff review before sending. Listed in detail below. Auditable.

1. Scope

This policy covers any use of artificial intelligence, machine-learning models, or AI-powered third-party services by LYMX Power, Inc. across getlymx.com, the customer wallet, the Business POS, our admin tools, and any AI-assisted internal operations.

It applies to all customers, Businesses, and Partners on the network. It supplements — and does not replace — our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

2. What we currently use AI for

Specific, narrow, transparent applications. Each one has a human accountable for it.

Internal — fraud

Anomaly detection on transactions

A small classification model flags transactions for human review (e.g., 5+ rapid redemptions across cities). The flag is a hint, not a decision.

Internal — support

Support ticket routing

Inbound tickets get auto-categorized (Customer / Business / Partner / Press / Investor) so they reach the right inbox faster. Always human-resolved.

Internal — drafts only

Email-reply drafts

Our support team uses AI to draft initial responses to common questions. A human reviews and edits every reply before sending.

Customer-facing

Help-center search

Semantic search across our help-center articles. Finds relevant articles based on meaning, not keyword match. No PII enters the search index.

Internal — analytics

Cohort analysis & insights

For our biz-analytics dashboard, AI summarizes patterns in aggregate, anonymized data — never with individual customers identified.

Internal — translation

Spanish translations

AI-assisted first-pass translations of customer help content into Spanish. A bilingual human reviews before publishing.

3. What we explicitly will not do

  • We will not use customer transaction data, wallet balances, or messages to train any AI model — neither our own nor a third-party provider's.
  • We will not sell, lease, or share customer data with AI training services, data brokers, or advertising AI platforms.
  • We will not use AI to make irreversible adverse decisions about a customer (account closure, wallet lockout) without a human reviewing the case.
  • We will not generate AI content that impersonates a Business or a Partner. AI-generated marketing material is always labeled internally and reviewed by a human staff member.
  • We will not deploy AI-powered tools without listing them on this page within 30 days of going live.
  • We will not claim our AI is more accurate than it is. Our fraud-flagging model has a known false-positive rate; we publish quarterly updates in our transparency report.

5. We do not train on your data

To make the no-training commitment concrete:

  • When we send any text to an AI provider (e.g., a support ticket for categorization), we use the provider's API setting that prevents the data from being used for model improvement.
  • We strip personally identifiable information (names, phone numbers, exact addresses) before sending text to AI providers wherever it's not strictly required for the task.
  • We do not maintain or use any AI fine-tuning dataset that contains real customer messages, real Business transactions, or real Partner communications.
  • If you sign up tomorrow and use LYMX for a year, nothing you type or do becomes part of any AI's training data — directly or indirectly.

6. Human-in-the-loop on adverse decisions

Some decisions affect you significantly: locking a wallet, closing an account, denying a Partner application, hiding a customer review. Our policy:

  1. AI may flag these cases (e.g., "this transaction looks unusual"). The flag is treated as a hint.
  2. A staff member reviews the flagged case before any user-affecting action is taken.
  3. The user is notified of the action and has a clear path to dispute it via the customer dispute flow.
  4. The reasoning and the human reviewer are logged for audit. Available on data-export request.

7. Your controls

You don't need to opt out of "AI training" because we don't train on your data. But here's what's in your control today:

  • Help-center search — you can opt out of semantic search by setting your help-center preference to "keyword only" in settings. Speed difference is minimal.
  • AI-drafted support replies — if you'd prefer a 100% human-written reply with no AI assist, add the phrase "please reply without AI assist" to your support ticket. We honor it.
  • Data export & deletion — full data export and account deletion remain available regardless of any AI features. Settings → My data.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll add new AI uses to this page within 30 days of deployment. Material changes (a new vendor, a new use case affecting customer-facing decisions) are announced via email to all customers and a banner on getlymx.com.

Minor edits (typos, new examples) are noted in the changelog.

Plain version

We use AI to make our own work faster — categorizing tickets, drafting support replies, finding the right help article. We do not use AI to learn anything about you that we can sell or train models on. If we ever change that, we'll tell you 30 days before, in plain English, and you can leave.
