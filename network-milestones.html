2024
Sep 2024

The "what if punch cards worked everywhere?" idea

Kenny is sitting at a Henderson coffee shop, looking at his stack of unused punch cards from 12 different local Businesses. Asks: "What if all of these were just one card?"

Oct 2024

First sketches + economics modeling

Kenny + Sienna (then a friend, soon to be first Partner) work through the math. Realize a fixed-redemption-value, network-wide, no-cash-out structure could work — and would dodge the regulatory complications of currency-like programs.

Nov 2024

10 Vegas Business owner interviews

Kenny calls 10 owners he knew personally. 9 say "yes, this could work." 1 says "you're insane." All 10 give detailed feedback that shapes the model.

Dec 2024

LLC formed — LYMX Power LLC

Las Vegas-based Nevada LLC. Kenny as 100% owner. Sienna as first Partner LOI. Founder + 1 Partner

2025
Q1 2025

Platform v1 prototype

Functional dashboard, customer wallet, basic POS integration with Square. Built scrappy.

Mar 2025

First Business activation — Brew & Bean Coffee

Henderson coffee shop becomes the first LYMX Business. Owner Daniel signs up after a 30-minute conversation with Kenny. 1 Business · 0 customers

Apr 2025

First customer wallet, first cross-redemption

A customer earns LYMX at Brew & Bean, then weeks later spends it at Sage & Stem Florist (Business #4). The mechanism works in production.

May 2025

5 Businesses live, $850 sign-up fee defined

Pricing crystallizes: $850 sign-up + $199/mo. 5 Businesses

Jun-Aug 2025

Toast, Clover, Lightspeed, Shopify integrations

5 POS integrations live. Customer wallet supports iOS + Android via Add to Home Screen.

Sep 2025

Launch 25 Business cohort opens

First 25 Businesses get founding-cohort recognition + locked perks. Filled in 18 days. 25 Businesses · 1,200 customers

Oct 2025

Public launch — getlymx.com goes live

Marketing site, Partner program, customer wallet all public. Ribbon-cutting event with mayor's office representative. 38 Businesses · 2,400 customers

Nov 2025

Top tier of Partner program. First 25 Partners to hit 5 Direct activations earn lifetime perks. 10 Partners signed up Day 1
