Quick FAQ

Is it really free?
Yes. Free admission, free LYMX welcome bonus to every customer who signs up at the door, free workshops. The Businesses on the network are running their own pricing — but their LYMX promotions are honored at the event.
Do I need to RSVP?
Not strictly required for entry, but yes — please RSVP. We use the count for catering, the 1,000-LYMX-per-customer welcome bonus pool, and to make sure we have enough chairs at the workshops.
What if I can't make it on Sep 12?
All the talks are recorded and posted on getlymx.com the following Monday. The customer welcome bonus is event-only — but you can still sign up and get the standard 100-LYMX welcome bonus any time at getlymx.com/join.
I'm a Business owner — is there a better track for me?
Yes. Look for the "Businesses" check-in desk at the entrance. We'll route you to the 2pm workshop and connect you with our Founding 25 Businesses for direct conversation. We also do a lot of cohort intake conversation that day.
Press credentials?
Email press@getlymx.com with your outlet by August 31. We send a press packet ahead of time and have a separate check-in desk on the day. Founder Q&A at 1 PM is on the record.
Will it rain?
It's Vegas in September — almost certainly not. If it does, Container Park has covered areas and we'll move the workshops indoors.
