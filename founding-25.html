★

🚀

Homepage feature

30 days at the top of getlymx.com after activation. Founding members rotate through the feature spot first.

📧

Network email blast

One-time launch email to all existing LYMX customers in Las Vegas — sent only for you. Hundreds to thousands of pre-engaged customers.

🔒

Locked grandfathered pricing

Whatever pricing you sign up at is locked for the duration of your subscription, even if LYMX raises prices for new Businesses later.

🤝

Quarterly founder summit

Direct access to the LYMX founder + other founding-25 owners. Roundtable discussion of the network's roadmap, your input shapes priorities.

📞

Direct line to support

Founding members get prioritized support — not a queue. Email or text the LYMX team directly for any issue.

📰

Press feature opportunity

Featured in LYMX's launch press release and ongoing media coverage as a Launch 25 cohort member.

🎁

500 LYMX gift

500 LYMX credited to your wallet at activation, redeemable at any other LYMX Business — courtesy of LYMX.
