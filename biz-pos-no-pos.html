FAQ

What if my cashier forgets to enter a transaction?

Customers can request retroactive credit by emailing you their receipt. You issue retroactive LYMX from the dashboard within 60 seconds. Or set up daily review: at end of shift, count cash + check LYMX dashboard, reconcile. Most tablet-fallback Businesses match within 1-2 transactions per day.

Can I switch from tablet to modern POS later?

Yes — anytime. If you upgrade your POS to Square/Toast/Clover/Lightspeed/Shopify, integrate it via Dashboard → Integrations. Your historical LYMX data carries over. The tablet becomes redundant; sell or repurpose.

What if my tablet's wifi cuts out?

LYMX Cashier App caches the last 24 hours of activity, so brief outages don't break it. For longer outages, write transactions on paper, enter them when wifi returns. Customers won't see the issue (their LYMX shows as "pending" briefly, then settles).

Do I need a separate phone number for the tablet?

No — your Business already has one LYMX account. The tablet is just another login, scoped to the "Cashier" role. Multiple devices can be logged in simultaneously (e.g., owner phone + counter tablet).

What if a customer doesn't have a phone with them?

Cashier types in the customer's phone number on the tablet (no need to scan QR). If it's a new customer, they get a welcome SMS to sign up + claim their LYMX. Old-fashioned customers without a smartphone can still earn — they just get LYMX held for them.

Is the tablet fallback considered "less professional"?

No — about 22% of LYMX Businesses use it. Some are very high-end (curated retail, fine dining family-owned). Customers don't notice; they just see "scan QR, earn LYMX." The mechanism is identical to a modern POS integration.
