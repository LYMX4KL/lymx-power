★ Core feature Earn + redeem

Show your QR. Done.

Your wallet's main job. A unique QR code you show to the cashier at any LYMX Business — they scan it, you earn or redeem LYMX. The QR refreshes every 10 seconds so screenshots can't be replayed.

  • Open wallet → tap "Earn" or "Spend" tab
  • Show the QR to the cashier
  • Confirm the amount on the screen if redeeming
  • Done — receipt shows LYMX activity

See exactly where your LYMX has been.

Your balance + every transaction since you joined. Filter by Business, by date range, or by transaction type (earned vs. redeemed). Export to CSV anytime.

  • Live balance with USD equivalent
  • Per-transaction detail with merchant + time
  • Monthly + yearly summaries
  • CSV export from Settings → Data

Catch live multiplier promos.

Businesses run multiplier promos to drive foot traffic — and you benefit. Earn 2×, 3×, or 5× LYMX on specific items, days, or events. The wallet shows you live promos near you.

  • Live "active promos" feed
  • Filter by category or location
  • Notification when a favorite Business runs a promo (opt-in)
  • See promo countdown — they expire
🎁 Referrals

Invite friends. Both of you earn.

Share your referral code. When a friend signs up + makes their first transaction, you both get 200 LYMX (= $2 each). No cap on how many friends.

  • Unique referral code per wallet
  • Share via text, email, or copy link
  • Track pending + earned referrals in dashboard
  • Bonus 500 LYMX for getting friends to refer their own friends (G2 referrals)

📱 No app

Add to home screen — it's like an app.

The LYMX wallet runs in your browser, but you can add it to your phone's home screen for one-tap access. Looks and behaves like a real app — but no App Store install.

  • iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
  • Android: tap menu → "Install app"
  • Works offline (cached) for browsing balance
  • Push notifications (optional, web standard)
