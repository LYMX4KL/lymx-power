LYMX Disclaimer

Plain-English disclosure of what the LYMX unit is and is not · Last updated: April 28, 2026 · Governing law: Nevada
In one sentence
The LYMX unit is a promotional credit redeemable at participating LYMX Businesses. It is not money, not cash, not legal tender, not a stored monetary balance, not cryptocurrency, and not a security.
It has a designated face value of $0.01 USD when accepted as payment at a participating Business. Outside the LYMX network it has no value.

✓ What LYMX IS

  • A promotional credit unit issued by LYMX Network to participating Businesses
  • Redeemable as payment at any participating LYMX Business at a face value of $0.01 per unit
  • Earnable when a customer makes a qualifying purchase at a participating Business that issues LYMX
  • Spendable across the network — earn at one Business, spend at any other
  • Tracked digitally in your LYMX wallet

✗ What LYMX is NOT

  • Not money, not cash, not US dollars, not legal tender
  • Not cryptocurrency, not a digital asset, not a coin or token in the legal sense
  • Not a stored monetary balance, not a prepaid card, not a debit instrument
  • Not a security; ownership of LYMX confers no investment rights
  • Not a gift card under applicable law (we believe — pending counsel review)
  • Not redeemable for cash, ACH, money order, or any other monetary instrument from any party including LYMX itself
  • Not transferable to other customers (except via LYMX's referral mechanism)
  • Not insured by FDIC, NCUA, SIPC, or any other deposit-insurance scheme

Who issues, sells, and buys back LYMX units

LYMX Network is the sole issuer of LYMX units. We sell units to participating Businesses at a wholesale price equal to 80% of face value ($0.008 per unit) when they issue them to a customer. We buy units back from participating Businesses at the same wholesale price ($0.008 per unit) when their customers redeem them at the Business's store.

The buy/sell relationship between LYMX and Businesses is structured as ordinary accounts payable / receivable — settled monthly. LYMX does not hold customer money, escrow funds, or operate a money-transmission service.

For customers

When you earn LYMX at a participating Business, the Business records your earning and your wallet balance increases. You can redeem your LYMX as payment at any other participating Business — your wallet balance decreases by the amount you redeem, and the receiving Business accepts the units at face value as payment.

What you can do with LYMX

  • Spend at any participating LYMX Business as payment (1 LYMX = $0.01 face value)
  • Earn additional LYMX at participating Businesses
  • Refer friends to earn referral bonuses (paid in LYMX)
  • Receive promotional LYMX from LYMX Network as part of welcome bonuses, referral rewards, etc.

What you cannot do

  • Convert LYMX to cash, ACH, gift card, money order, or any other monetary instrument from LYMX or any Business
  • Sell or transfer your LYMX balance to another customer
  • Use LYMX outside the network of participating LYMX Businesses

If a Business closes

Your LYMX is held in your wallet at LYMX Network — not at any individual Business. If a Business that issued you LYMX closes or leaves the network, your LYMX remains spendable at every other LYMX Business.

If LYMX Network shuts down

If LYMX Network were to wind down operations, we would give at least 90 days' notice for customers to redeem outstanding LYMX at participating Businesses before the network closes. After that point, LYMX units have no value. Customers do not have a contractual right to a cash buyout.

For Businesses

When you sign up as a participating Business, you agree to the Business Activation Agreement, which incorporates this Disclaimer. The buy/sell terms — selling units to LYMX at $0.008, buying back from LYMX at $0.008 — are documented in your monthly settlement statement.

Your participation does not make you a money transmitter, prepaid issuer, or stored-value issuer. You are a Business that buys promotional credit units from LYMX and redeems them as payment from customers, then sells them back to LYMX.

For Partners

LYMX-denominated commissions paid to Partners are credited to the Partner's network LYMX balance. Like all LYMX, these commissions:

  • Are spendable at any participating LYMX Business at face value
  • Are not convertible to cash, ACH, or any monetary instrument from LYMX
  • Are not securities and confer no investment rights

Cash compensation to Partners (sign-up bonuses + monthly subscription overrides) is paid via ACH and is fully convertible. The LYMX-denominated component is separate and network-only.

No FDIC / SIPC insurance

LYMX is not a bank, not a broker, not an investment company. Your LYMX balance is not insured by the FDIC, NCUA, SIPC, or any other deposit insurance scheme. The unit is a promotional credit — not a deposit, not a security.

No earnings or reward guarantees

LYMX does not guarantee that any specific amount of LYMX will be issued to you, nor does the network guarantee specific levels of customer traffic to any Business. The benefits of participating in the network depend on factors specific to each customer, Business, and Partner.

Partners specifically: LYMX makes no income guarantees, and Partners may not represent the LYMX program in any way that suggests guaranteed earnings or specific income levels. Compensation depends entirely on the Partner's own activity and the activity of Businesses they recruit.

Regulatory positioning

LYMX has structured the LYMX unit and the buy/sell mechanic with the intent of remaining outside the scope of:

  • Federal money-transmission regulation (31 CFR 1010.100(ff)(5))
  • Nevada Money Transmission Act (NRS Chapter 671)
  • Stored-value / prepaid card regulation
  • Securities regulation

This positioning is based on legal analysis and is subject to ongoing regulatory review. We may change the structure if required by counsel or regulators. Material changes will be disclosed and announced in advance.
