🛠️ Troubleshooting

📱
My SMS code didn't arrive
Wait 60 seconds, then "Send again". Check spam. If still missing, email support.
›
⬢
QR not scanning at checkout
Increase screen brightness, hold steady, ask cashier to retry. If fails, give phone number instead.
›
💸
Missing LYMX from a purchase
Wait 5 minutes. If still missing, send your receipt and customer ID to support.
›
🔒
Locked out of my account
Tap "Forgot password" or contact support with your registered phone number.
›
🌐
Browser compatibility
Latest Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge supported. Older browsers may have issues.
›
🔁
Refunded purchase — what happens to LYMX?
LYMX issued for that purchase is reversed automatically within 24 hrs.
›
