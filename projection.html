LYMX is a promotional credit unit issued, sold to, and bought back by LYMX exclusively within the partner ecosystem. LYMX commission earned by Partners is spendable across the partner network, not convertible to cash. LYMX is not cryptocurrency and not a stored monetary balance. Sign-up bonus and monthly subscription overrides are paid in cash. Projections are estimates based on inputs and current MGC rates; actual earnings may differ. Sign-up bonus is paid as $250 at day 7 + $250 at day 30 (anti-fraud).
