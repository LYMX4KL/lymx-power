How to send from this address in Gmail (5 min, one-time setup) ▾
  1. Open Gmail → ⚙️ gear → See all settings → Accounts and Import.
  2. In the "Send mail as" section, click Add another email address.
  3. Name: your full name · Email: your @getlymx.com · Uncheck "Treat as alias". Click Next Step.
  4. SMTP Server: email-smtp.us-east-1.amazonaws.com · Port: 587
  5. Username + Password: find them in your welcome email from LYMX <hello@getlymx.com> — search Gmail for "Send mail as" or your @getlymx.com address. They'll be in a table near the bottom.
  6. Connection: Secured connection using TLS (default). Click Add Account.
  7. Gmail sends a verification email to your @getlymx.com address. Because of LYMX's email forwarding, it lands in your regular Gmail inbox in seconds. Click the verification link.
  8. Done. When composing, the From: dropdown lets you pick your @getlymx.com address.

Lost the welcome email? Search Gmail for: from:hello@getlymx.com Welcome · Or contact support to re-send.
