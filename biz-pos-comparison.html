Which POS works best with LYMX?

5 major POS platforms have direct LYMX integrations. They're all capable — but each has trade-offs. Pick the right one based on your category and what you already use.

The 30-second answer

If you don't want to read the whole comparison, here's the rule of thumb.

Café, retail, services→ Square
Restaurant (full-service)→ Toast
Quick-service / counter→ Clover
Independent retail / boutique→ Lightspeed
Online + in-store→ Shopify
Already have a POS→ Use what you have

Square

The most popular LYMX integration
  • Fastest setup (12 min)
  • Highest stability rating (4.9/5)
  • Supports retail, café, services equally well
  • Great for first-time POS users
  • Built-in customer matching
Best for: Cafés, salons, gyms, retail boutiques. Anything where the POS is the central tool.
See Square setup →
🍞

Toast

Restaurant-grade integration
  • Course-by-course LYMX awarding
  • Tip-aware (subtotal-only)
  • Multi-guest split checks
  • Server-level reporting
  • Toast Pay tableside flow integrated
Best for: Full-service restaurants, fine dining, breweries with kitchens. Anywhere the check is complex.
See Toast setup →
🍀

Clover

App Market simplicity
  • App Market install (no separate sign-in)
  • Works on Mini, Mobile, Station, Flex
  • Multi-merchant rollup
  • Coexists with Clover Loyalty
  • Solid for QSR + counter retail
Best for: Quick-service restaurants, food trucks, fast-casual, counter retail. Multi-tier hardware shops.
See Clover setup →
LS

Lightspeed

Inventory-aware power
  • SKU-level multipliers (most granular)
  • Stock-aware auto-disable on out-of-stock
  • Brand attribution (multi-brand stores)
  • Both Retail (X-Series) + Restaurant (K-Series)
  • Best returns reconciliation
Best for: Independent retailers with deep inventory — bookstores, music shops, specialty retail, boutiques.
See Lightspeed setup →
🛍️

Shopify

Online + in-store bridge
  • Earn online → redeem in-store (and vice versa)
  • Same wallet, both worlds
  • Subscription product support
  • Abandoned cart triggers
  • Shopify Plus checkout extensibility
Best for: Direct-to-consumer brands with both an online store and physical retail. Hybrid retailers.
See Shopify setup →
