⚙️ What we can do with your data

Granular controls — you can turn each one on or off independently.

Email me about my account

Required for account security. Includes settlement statements, dispute updates, and security alerts.

Email me about new Businesses on the network

Notify when a new LYMX Business opens near you. Roughly once a month.

Email me about promotions

Multipliers, Happy Hours, and Founding-Business email blasts. Up to 2 per week.

SMS for transaction confirmations

Text after every earn or redemption. Helpful for fraud detection.

Show me in the public Partner / customer leaderboard

If you opt out, your name won't appear. Default: opt-in only after you crack the top 50.

Help-center semantic search

Uses an AI service to find articles by meaning. Turn off for keyword-only search. More on AI use.

Anonymous analytics

Helps us see which pages help vs. confuse. We use Plausible (cookieless, no individual tracking).

⚠️ Delete my account

Permanent. We can restore within 7 days if you change your mind — after that, the data is gone.

What we'll delete: profile, wallet balance, transaction history, reviews, support tickets, referrals, login history.

What we'll keep (anonymized): aggregate transaction counts (for our financial records), per-Business sales tax records (legal requirement, 7 years).

Timeline: production deletion within 30 days · backup deletion within 90 days · final confirmation by email.
