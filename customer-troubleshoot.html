Something not working?

Most LYMX issues fix themselves with one of three things: refresh the page, sign out and back in, or wait 60 seconds. The list below covers what to do when those don't work.

All issues App / wallet Payment / QR Balance Business issues

Wallet won't load — blank page or spinning

App
Symptom: wallet.lymx.com loads to a blank screen, or the spinner never stops.
Fix in 60 seconds
  1. Hard refresh: hold Shift + tap reload, or close and reopen the browser tab
  2. Check status.lymx.com — if our network is down, this isn't your issue
  3. Try a different browser (Safari → Chrome or vice versa)
  4. Clear browser cache for lymx.com

QR code won't scan at the register

Payment
Symptom: Cashier holds scanner over your phone, nothing happens.
Fix in 30 seconds
  1. Increase your phone brightness all the way up (low brightness = scanner can't read)
  2. Wipe your screen — fingerprints break QR contrast
  3. Tap your phone screen so it doesn't dim mid-scan
  4. Hold phone flat, scanner 4-6 inches above
  5. If still failing — give cashier your phone number instead. Same effect.

I paid but no LYMX appeared in my wallet

Balance
Symptom: You paid 30+ minutes ago and your balance hasn't updated.
Fix steps
  1. Pull-to-refresh the wallet (drag down on home screen)
  2. Check if the Business actually scanned your QR — sometimes cashier forgets. Look at your purchase receipt; if no LYMX line item, it wasn't scanned.
  3. If Business scanned but no LYMX: file a "Missing LYMX claim" via wallet → Help → Missing LYMX. Include receipt photo. We resolve within 24 hours.
  4. Note: most Businesses sync to LYMX in real-time. A few sync once per day at midnight — your LYMX appears next morning. Check the Business' integration page if you're unsure.
Still missing after 24 hours? Email missing@lymx.com with receipt photo. We track down where the issuance failed.

I was double-charged in LYMX

Balance
Symptom: Your balance dropped twice for what should have been a single redemption.
Fix steps
  1. Check transaction history (wallet → History) — confirm two separate debits
  2. If both at the same Business at the same minute, that's a duplicate charge — file dispute via customer-dispute.html
  3. Refund typically resolves within 3 business days after Business confirms the duplicate
Need it faster? Show the cashier the duplicate in your history — most Businesses can reverse the second charge on the spot from their POS.

Business says "we don't accept LYMX" but they're on the network

Business
Symptom: The Business map shows them as a LYMX Business but the cashier says no.
Likely causes (in order)
  1. Cashier is new and doesn't know — ask for the manager
  2. Business is leaving the network — they should still accept (90-day wind-down rule). If they refuse, file a complaint via customer-dispute.html
  3. POS integration is down at that location today — Business should manually note your purchase and apply LYMX retroactively
  4. Map data is stale — the Business may have actually left more than 90 days ago. Check their listing — should show "Inactive" badge.

Wallet says "session expired" — won't let me log back in

App
Symptom: You log in, screen flashes, "session expired" appears immediately.
Fix steps
  1. This is almost always a system clock issue — your phone's time/date is off
  2. Settings → General → Date & Time → enable "Set automatically"
  3. Refresh wallet, log in again
  4. If it persists, clear browser cookies for lymx.com — then log in

I never get the verification text

App
Symptom: You request a verification code, never receive a text.
Fix steps
  1. Wait 90 seconds — sometimes carrier-side delay
  2. Check spam/blocked messages folder — some carriers route shortcodes there
  3. Confirm you typed your phone number correctly (no extra digit, country code if international)
  4. Try voice call instead — tap "Call instead" on the verification screen, we read the code aloud
  5. If your carrier blocks shortcodes (some prepaid carriers do): contact support@lymx.com for manual verification

"Insufficient LYMX" but my balance shows enough

Balance
Symptom: You have 5,000 LYMX, you try to redeem 3,000, get error.
Likely causes
  1. Some of your LYMX is "pending" (recently issued, not yet settled — usually clears in 24 hours). Pending LYMX shows in balance but can't be spent.
  2. Wallet → tap balance → see "Available" vs "Pending" breakdown
  3. Wait 24 hours, retry
  4. If still failing with no pending balance: it's a sync issue. Pull-to-refresh, then retry.

Promo I saw earlier disappeared from my wallet

Business
Symptom: A 2x LYMX promo was advertised yesterday — gone today.
Likely causes
  1. Promo was time-limited (most are 1-7 days)
  2. Promo had a daily cap and the Business hit it
  3. Business ended the promo early — within their right
  4. You can see all live promos at wallet → "Promos near me" — if you don't see it, it's not active

I don't recognize a transaction in my history

Balance
Symptom: Random Business name showing a charge you don't remember.
Fix steps
  1. Check the Business' real-world name vs LYMX name — sometimes "Maria's Cafe LLC" shows as "Cafe Maria"
  2. Check date/time — sometimes friend used your phone while you were at a Business, or you forgot a small purchase
  3. If truly unauthorized: file a dispute within 30 days. We refund verified unauthorized activity.
  4. Lock your wallet first if you think someone has access — see account recovery

Add to Home Screen doesn't work / disappears

App
Symptom: You added the wallet icon, now it's gone — or it never installed.
Fix steps
  1. iOS: must use Safari (not Chrome) to "Add to Home Screen." Chrome adds bookmarks but not home-screen icons.
  2. Android: in Chrome, must tap ⋮ → "Install app" (not "Add to Home Screen")
  3. If icon disappeared: phone may have offloaded unused apps. iOS Settings → App Store → Offload Unused Apps → off.

Wallet super slow on weekends

App
Symptom: Page loads in 1 second on weekday, 8 seconds on Saturday night.
Likely causes
  1. Network congestion — wallet uses cellular when out and about, Saturday in Vegas means tower congestion. Switch to wifi if possible.
  2. Map view is heavy — if you always land on map, switch home page to "Recent" (Settings → Default home tab).
  3. Old phone — the wallet works on phones back to iOS 14 / Android 9 but performs best on newer hardware.

Can I send my LYMX to a friend's wallet?

Balance
Short answer: No — LYMX is not transferable between customers.
Why, and what to do instead
  1. LYMX is a personal loyalty balance, not money. Customer-to-customer transfers aren't allowed (anti-fraud and regulatory — by design), so there's no "send to a phone number" feature.
  2. Want to treat someone? Invite them with your referral link — when they sign up and make their first purchase, you both earn a bonus.
  3. Or take them to a LYMX Business and pay the bill — they sign up at the counter (30 seconds) and earn their own LYMX on the visit.

App/wallet feels stuck in another language

App
Symptom: Wallet showing in Spanish (or another language) when you want English.
Fix steps
  1. The wallet auto-detects browser language. Settings → Language → English (US)
  2. If your phone is set to Spanish system-wide, the auto-detect picks Spanish. Override in wallet settings.
  3. We support: English, Spanish, Mandarin, Cantonese, Vietnamese, Tagalog, Korean (rolling out 2026)

I'm getting too many LYMX notifications

App
Symptom: Promo notifications, transaction alerts, "Business near you" pings — overwhelmed.
Fix steps
  1. Settings → Notifications → choose level: "Critical only" / "Promos & critical" / "All"
  2. Critical-only = transaction confirmations + security alerts
  3. Or turn off all browser notifications for lymx.com in your phone settings
