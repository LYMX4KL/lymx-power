Three reasons we win the comparison

🌐

The network effect

Customers earn at one Business and spend at any other in the network. Stocard and the rest are stand-alone programs — your points are worthless if you don't return to the same store.

💵

You keep more of the value

Most loyalty programs profit on point breakage (70-90% never get redeemed). LYMX has no expiration. The cost to you is the LYMX customers actually use — which means it's only paid when it's working.

⚡

Setup in a day, not a month

No tablet rental. No 3-year contract. No POS replacement. Activate the LYMX Web POS on an iPad you already own and you're live by lunch.

Which is right for you?

Honest answer: not LYMX in every case. Here's our take on when each tool wins.

☕

Independent café, salon, retail

You run 1-3 locations and want repeat customers. The network effect is your unfair advantage — every other LYMX Business sends you new traffic.

Best fit: LYMX
🏢

Already deeply on Square or Toast

If you've built your whole stack on Square POS or Toast and you don't want a second tool, the bundled loyalty add-on is a fine "good enough."

Best fit: Square Loyalty / Toast Loyalty
📱

Customer wants to see all loyalty cards in one place

Stocard is genuinely useful here — it's a wallet aggregator, not a Business loyalty tool. We'd recommend it as a complement to LYMX, not a replacement.

Best fit: Stocard (as a customer)
📍

Heavy Yelp traffic with paid ads

If 60% of your new customers come from Yelp search, Yelp Connect's ad spend may convert better than loyalty alone. Pair LYMX with it.

Best fit: Yelp Connect (for ads), LYMX (for retention)
🍕

Multi-location restaurant chain (3+)

Toast Loyalty is the closest match if you're already on Toast POS. LYMX is competitive but more compelling once we have a Phase-3 nationwide network.

Best fit: Toast Loyalty (today), LYMX (Phase 3)
🛒

Local Business that grew up alongside the LYMX network

This is the dream scenario for us. You sign up as Founding 25, lock in the launch package, and your customers grow with the network for years.

Best fit: LYMX
