Why this role exists

I'm Kenny, founder of LYMX Power. I've been the only engineer on the codebase for the last 14 months, and the product is past the point where one person can be the bottleneck. We have 13 active Businesses, 8,400 customers, $750K of our $1.5M seed closed, and a public launch event scheduled for September.

I'm hiring my first full-time engineer — not to replace me, but to take over enough of the codebase that I can spend 80% of my time on Business + Partner GTM. You'd own the wallet, POS, and settlement engine end-to-end, and we'd pair on the harder architectural calls.

"This is a real founding-engineer role. You'll have the kind of leverage that disappears once a company is past 20 people."

What you'd be working on

The first 90 days, roughly:

  • Square POS native integration. We have a receipt-scan fallback today. Native sync is targeted for end of Q2. You'd own the build.
  • Public Node + Python SDKs for our API. Currently we have raw HTTP docs. Goal: npm install @lymx/node works by Q3.
  • Wallet QR-refresh accessibility fix. Screen reader doesn't announce QR refresh today. Known issue, due May.
  • The webhook reliability work. One outage in November because of an internal Stripe-rate-limit collision. Build the fix.
  • Pair with Kenny on architecture. Settlement v3 schema, multi-city expansion, the Toast integration.

What we're looking for

Concrete things that would make you a strong candidate:

  • 5+ years shipping production code in a small team. Not "5 years at a FAANG" — 5 years where you've owned outcomes, not just tickets.
  • Full-stack comfort. Our stack is React + Node + Postgres + Stripe + Twilio + Supabase. We don't need you to be expert at all of it on day 1, but you should be able to ship in any of them within 2 weeks.
  • Care about the customer. You'll write code that touches a real customer's wallet balance the day it ships. That should excite you, not stress you out.
  • Comfortable with marketplace + fintech ambiguity. Settlement, fraud signals, multi-tenant data — you'll be making calls on these every week. Prior context here is a plus.
  • You write things down. When you ship, you also write the post-mortem, the changelog entry, and the doc update. Not because we make you — because you would do it on your own.

Nice to have, not required

  • Prior loyalty / rewards / payments experience. (No one applying has it. We don't expect you to either.)
  • Experience with iPad-based POS systems.
  • Spanish-language fluency (we have a Spanish translation in progress).
  • Las Vegas based — but we're flexible.

Compensation & benefits

$160K – $200K base + 0.5% – 1.0% equity

Base depends on experience and how much pairing-with-Kenny you take on. Equity is 4-year vest, 1-year cliff. We're a Delaware C-corp on a YC-style SAFE.

Plus:

  • Health, dental, vision — fully covered for you, 80% for dependents
  • 4 weeks PTO — actually take it
  • Home-office stipend — $2,000 for setup, $200/mo ongoing
  • Las Vegas office — Container Park co-working desk, optional
  • One Founding-Business meal/week — paid in LYMX. Mandatory ;)
