Pricing & cost 5 questions

How much does LYMX really cost?

$850 one-time + $199/month after a 3-month free trial. The $850 includes the 3 months free, your homepage feature spot at launch, an email blast to all local LYMX customers, and your sign-up. After month 3, it's $199/mo flat. Plus the LYMX wholesale rate ($0.008 per LYMX issued — typically $20–40/mo for a small café, scaling with sales).

What's the wholesale rate and how does it work?

You decide your LYMX issuance rate at activation. Common choices are 5 LYMX per $1 (5% effective return, like a generous loyalty program) or 3 LYMX per $1 (3%, more conservative). Your Partner walks you through the decision during intake — and shows what your competitors offer. Once locked, the rate doesn't change without re-activation. Customers redeem at the network-wide $0.01 per LYMX. When LYMX is redeemed at another Business, you pay only 80% of face value at settlement (the 20% is an automatic referral discount). Plus a 3% processing fee on every redemption (charged in LYMX, auto-deducted at settlement).

Can I run LYMX alongside my existing loyalty program?

Yes. Most LYMX Businesses run both for the first 30–60 days as a transition. After that, ~85% of Businesses retire their old loyalty program because LYMX covers the same use case plus delivers network referrals. Square Loyalty, Toast Loyalty, Fivestars, and Belly are all compatible — they don't interfere with LYMX.

What's the ROI typically?

For an active café (~150 cust/day, $9 avg ticket), the typical month-2 ROI is 8–12× the LYMX cost (subscription + wholesale). Restaurants and retail tend to higher ROIs (12–25×) because of the larger ticket sizes. Higher-traffic Businesses see 20×+ in steady state. Use the ROI calculator to model your specific situation.

Are there hidden fees I should know about?

No. The full price is: $850 (with included perks) + $199/mo + $0.008/LYMX issued. We don't charge transaction fees, scan fees, account-setup fees, or upgrade tiers. Any new feature we add is included. Read our complete pricing page for the full breakdown.

Getting started 5 questions

How long does setup take?

Account creation + integration: ~12 minutes for Square or ~18 minutes for Toast. Cashier training: 5 minutes per person. Soft launch: 1 day. Full ramp to steady state: 4–6 weeks. See the onboarding checklist for the exact 12-step flow.

Do I need to replace my POS?

No. LYMX layers on top of your existing POS. We integrate with Square and Toast directly. For other POS systems (Clover, Lightspeed), we have a CSV-based reconciliation flow and a public API. See the integrations page.

What happens to my existing customers' data?

Your existing customer data stays in your POS. LYMX only sees customer data when a LYMX customer chooses to enter their phone number at your checkout — and even then we only see their name, phone, and transaction. We never see card numbers or billing addresses.

Do my employees need to learn anything?

About 5 minutes of new flow. The new step at checkout is: ask the customer "do you have LYMX?" → if yes, scan their QR. That's it. We have a 5-min cashier training video they can watch.

Can I get help with setup?

Yes — for the first 30 days you have priority support including a dedicated Slack channel for new Businesses. We also have an option for on-site help on launch day if you're in Las Vegas (free for Launch 25, $200 for others). See the Day 1 launch playbook.

Day-to-day operations 5 questions

How do customers know to use LYMX at my register?

Three ways. First, signage — we send a free print kit with window clings and table tents. Second, in your store on the LYMX browse map. Third, customers who earn LYMX at one Business get prompted to use it at others nearby. The first two get you started; the third compounds.

What if a customer's QR doesn't scan?

Cashier can manually enter the customer's phone number instead. The wallet looks up by phone. Takes ~10 sec. We document this in the cashier training video.

Can I run promotions/multipliers?

Yes — that's one of LYMX's biggest features. Promotions can be time-of-day, day-of-week, item-specific, customer-specific (first-timers, birthdays), or category-wide. The annual playbook has 12 months of recipe-style promo ideas.

What does my dashboard show me?

Real-time transaction count, daily/weekly/monthly revenue lift attributable to LYMX, customer adoption rate, top-performing menu items, multiplier ROI, peer benchmarks. Also includes individual customer-level history (with privacy protections) and settlement reports. See the full analytics page.

Will LYMX customers spend more or less than non-LYMX customers?

More — typically 12–18% more per ticket. The reason: most LYMX customers have a positive balance and treat LYMX redemptions as "found money," which lowers their psychological friction on add-ons. They also visit more often (3.8 visits/mo vs 2.6 for non-LYMX customers in cafés).

Multi-location 3 questions

Do I pay $199/mo per location?

No — multi-location is much cheaper than that. Your first location is $199/mo. Each additional location is just $50/mo. So 3 locations = $199 + $50 + $50 = $299/mo. 5 locations = $199 + (4 × $50) = $399/mo. Centralized LYMX ledger across all locations. Beyond ~10 locations talk to us about Enterprise tier.

Can I have a single LYMX program across all my locations?

Yes. You can either (a) have each location issue + redeem independently (good for Businesses with different concepts), or (b) consolidate everything as one Business with multiple locations (good for chain-style operations). LYMX customers see them as the same Business in mode (b).

What about chains with 10+ locations?

That's our Enterprise tier. We work with 5+ regional chains. Custom pricing, dedicated account management, custom analytics, multi-store API support. Contact us at enterprise@getlymx.com.

If I want to leave 3 questions

Can I cancel? What's the refund policy?

Yes — anytime through your dashboard. The $850 is partially refundable within 30 days of sign-up (we keep $250 to cover platform setup costs). After that: when you submit cancellation, your outstanding LYMX balance is auto-capped (you can't issue new LYMX during the wind-down). Your getlymx.com listing flips to a "Leaving the LYMX network — still accepting LYMX" banner with days remaining. You continue accepting LYMX for 90 days OR until your balance hits $0 — whichever comes first. After 90 days, listing is removed from the public site. Read our full refund policy.

What happens to my customers' LYMX if I leave?

Customer LYMX balances are network-wide — they continue to be spendable at all OTHER LYMX Businesses. Your departure doesn't affect their balance. You stop issuing new LYMX immediately upon cancellation; existing redemptions you've collected continue to settle through to the cancellation date.

What if I sell my Business?

The LYMX account transfers as part of the asset sale — the new owner takes over the Business's LYMX history, customer base, and current balance. Condition: the new owner stays in the LYMX network. If they decline LYMX, the standard 90-day cancellation window applies. Update us at hello@getlymx.com at least 30 days before the sale closes.

Can I pause instead of cancel?

Yes — up to 60 days. Useful for seasonal Businesses or owners taking extended vacation. Pause means: no monthly subscription billed, customers can't redeem LYMX at your Business during pause, your menu & settings preserved. Resume anytime. Pause requests via support.

Technical questions 3 questions

What POS systems do you support?

Direct integration: Square, Toast. Beta: Clover, Lightspeed, Aloha. CSV reconciliation: any system that can export daily sales. Public API for everything else: api.html. See the full integrations list.

How does data sync work? Real-time?

Transactions sync real-time (within 30 seconds). Menu items sync every 5 minutes. Customer records hourly. Refunds & voids real-time. If your POS is offline, transactions queue and replay on reconnection — nothing is lost.

Is my data encrypted? Are you SOC 2 compliant?

All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). SOC 2 Type 2 certification target: Q4 2026 (we're in audit now). PCI compliance: we never see card data — Square / Stripe handle that. See security page for the full breakdown.
