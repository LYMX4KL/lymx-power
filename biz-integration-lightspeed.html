How to connect Lightspeed

Step-by-step setup. ⏱️ Total: ~20 min

1

Open Lightspeed App Marketplace

Sign in to your Lightspeed account → click App Marketplace in the sidebar. Search for "LYMX" and click Install.

~2 min
2

Authorize the integration

Lightspeed will ask permission for read access to inventory + customers, write access to sales. Approve. This happens once.

~1 min
3

Choose your variant (Retail or Restaurant)

If you have both, choose which to connect. Most Businesses pick one. You can connect both later via additional integrations.

~1 min
4

Map categories to LYMX multipliers

Walk through your top-level categories. Set default multiplier per category. For specific SKUs you want to boost individually, you can configure those in the LYMX dashboard later.

~8 min
5

Test with a $1 transaction

Ring up a test sale on your Lightspeed POS. Confirm LYMX issuance shows on the receipt + dashboard. Refund the transaction to confirm reversal.

~3 min
6

Enable multi-location (if applicable)

If you have multiple Lightspeed locations, enable cross-location inventory sync in Integrations → Lightspeed → Multi-location. Decides whether each store has independent LYMX or shared.

~2 min
7

Train staff (5 min)

Standard LYMX cashier training applies. Lightspeed's POS UI is similar to Square's for the cashier — just additional buttons during checkout. Watch the cashier video.

~5 min

Frequently asked questions

Does this work with Lightspeed eCom (Lightspeed's online store)?

Yes. Online sales sync the same way. Customers can apply LYMX at the eCom checkout — wallet phone number entry is added to the checkout flow automatically.

What about Lightspeed Golf, Salon, or other vertical-specific products?

Currently supported: Retail (X-Series) and Restaurant (K-Series). Lightspeed Golf, Salon, and Bike are on the roadmap for Q3–Q4 2026. Contact us if you'd like early access.

How does this handle Lightspeed's "merchant fees" pricing tier?

Lightspeed's per-transaction fees are independent of LYMX. We don't charge transaction fees. The LYMX wholesale rate ($0.008 per LYMX) is the only LYMX-specific cost beyond your monthly subscription.

What if I have a custom Lightspeed integration with a 3rd-party tool?

The LYMX integration shouldn't conflict — we use Lightspeed's standard API, which doesn't lock out other integrations. Test in your sandbox environment first if you have a custom setup.

How are partial refunds handled?

If you process a partial refund in Lightspeed, LYMX automatically reverses the proportional issuance. Example: customer paid $40, earned 200 LYMX. You refund $20. LYMX reverses 100 LYMX automatically.

Can I pause LYMX without uninstalling?

Yes. In Lightspeed App Marketplace → LYMX → toggle "Active" off. Your inventory + transactions stop syncing. Re-enable anytime — all settings preserved.
