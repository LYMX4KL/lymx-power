LYMX Business Terms

Plain-English explanation of the agreement between you (a "Business") and LYMX Power Inc. Read the whole thing — there's no fine print to hide.

Effective: 2026-05-17 · Last updated: 2026-05-17 · Contact: hello@getlymx.com
The short version. You pay a one-time setup fee, then a monthly subscription. In exchange, you can issue LYMX (a loyalty credit) to your customers, and you agree to honor LYMX from any LYMX customer at the published redemption rate. Either party can leave with notice. Your data is yours; we never sell it.

1. What LYMX is — and isn't

LYMX is a promotional credit unit. 1 LYMX = $0.01 in redemption value at any participating LYMX Business. LYMX is not currency, not cryptocurrency, and not a stored monetary balance. Customers cannot withdraw LYMX as cash. Acceptance and redemption are governed by the rules in this agreement and the per-Business configuration you set.

3. Founding 25

The first 25 Businesses to sign up are the "Founding 25" — they receive a permanent fee waiver on the monthly subscription, a homepage feature spot, and a first-mover positioning in the network. The setup fee still applies.

4. Your obligations as a Business

  • Honor LYMX from any LYMX customer at the published redemption rate, regardless of which Business issued the LYMX. This is the core of the network.
  • Issue LYMX as advertised. Don't promote a higher rate publicly than you configured.
  • Don't pay your own staff in LYMX as a wage substitute. LYMX is a customer rewards instrument.
  • Don't create fraudulent issuances (e.g., to yourself, family, blocklisted addresses). Our anti-fraud triggers will flag and reverse these.
  • Provide accurate business information in your profile.

5. LYMX's obligations

  • Maintain the LYMX network so customers can redeem their LYMX at any participating Business.
  • Honor 1 LYMX = $0.01 redemption value. We will not silently devalue.
  • Provide a working dashboard, support, and integration documentation.
  • Settle inter-Business redemptions transparently — when a customer redeems LYMX they earned elsewhere at your store, LYMX reimburses you for the redemption.
  • Send you a monthly statement.

6. Data

Your customer data is yours. We don't sell it to third parties. We use it to (a) run the LYMX network, (b) settle redemptions, (c) detect fraud, and (d) provide you with aggregated insights about your business. You can request export or deletion of your business's data at any time by emailing hello@getlymx.com.

Specific privacy details: see our Privacy Policy.

7. Approval, suspension, termination

LYMX may decline new Business applications at its discretion. Once approved, your account remains active for as long as you pay the subscription and follow this agreement. LYMX may suspend your account for fraud, repeated chargebacks, or material breach of section 4 above, with notice. Either party can terminate the agreement with 30 days written notice. On termination, LYMX honors all outstanding customer LYMX redemptions; you stop earning new business from the network.

8. Refunds

During the 3-month free trial, you can cancel any time with no charge. The setup fee is refundable in full if cancelled within the first 14 days of approval and you have not issued any LYMX. After 14 days, the setup fee is non-refundable. Monthly fees are non-refundable except as required by law.

9. Liability

LYMX's total liability is capped at the fees you paid in the trailing 12 months. LYMX is not liable for indirect or consequential damages. LYMX is not a bank, a money transmitter, or a payment processor. We provide promotional rewards software.

10. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms; we will email all Businesses 30 days in advance of any change. If you don't agree, you can terminate without penalty during the 30-day window.

11. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Nevada, USA. Disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration in Las Vegas, NV, except either party may seek injunctive relief in court.
