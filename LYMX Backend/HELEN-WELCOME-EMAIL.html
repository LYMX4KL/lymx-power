Setting up home-address clock-in for Dave and Rachel

Dave and Rachel are set to clock in only from their home address (per Kenny's policy). The geofence is 200 metres — about one city block. If they try to punch in from a coffee shop across town, the system will refuse. This protects payroll integrity.

  1. Open admin-staff-locations.html.
  2. For each person:
    • Paste their full home address into the "Home address" box, then click Lookup — the latitude and longitude will fill in automatically.
    • Leave "Geofence radius" at 200 (metres). If their home is in a large building or you need a slightly wider buffer, bump it up to 300-400.
    • Leave "Remote allowed" off (strict mode). Only check it for staff who legitimately need to punch from a different location (rare).
    • Click Save.
  3. Forward this short instruction to Dave and Rachel:
