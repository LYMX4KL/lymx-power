How money flows

You → LYMX: $199/month after your 3-month trial. Charged automatically; you'll get a Stripe invoice each month. Plus you buy LYMX units from us at $0.008 per LYMX (80% of face value) to issue to your customers.

LYMX → You: When customers redeem LYMX at your business, LYMX buys those units back from you at the same $0.008 per LYMX. The 20% gap (face value vs buy-back) is your effective discount expense. Settled monthly on the 5th business day via ACH to the bank you connect here.
