How money flows

You → LYMX: $199/month after your 3-month trial. Charged automatically; you'll get a Stripe invoice each month.

LYMX → You: When a customer redeems LYMX they earned elsewhere at your business, LYMX sends you the cash equivalent (1 LYMX = $0.01). Settled weekly via ACH to the bank you connect on this page.
