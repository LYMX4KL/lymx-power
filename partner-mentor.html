How it works

3 phases. 90 days. Hour-a-week commitment.

1

Apply & get matched

Tell us your goals, geography, and category focus. We match you with a Master Partner whose path most closely aligns with where you want to go.

2

30-min weekly calls

One call per week with your mentor. They share what's worked, listen to what's not, push you when needed. You bring an agenda; they bring perspective.

3

Graduate & pay it forward

After 90 days, you're done. Some mentees stay in touch. Some go on to mentor others themselves once they hit Active or Master tier.

What you get from a mentor

It's not training (that's the academy). It's not coaching (that's the success team). It's something else.

🎯

Specific tactical advice

"Don't pitch that prospect this way — try this instead." Mentors share pattern-matched advice based on hundreds of pitches they've personally done.

🔄

Real prospect feedback

Bring an actual prospect to the call. Mentor helps you research them, build a custom pitch, role-play it. Higher conversion than going alone.

🧠

Pattern recognition

"I've seen this 50 times — here's what'll happen next." Mentors save you from making mistakes you don't even know you're about to make.

⏰

Accountability & cadence

Weekly call structure forces you to keep moving. Most new Partners stall in month 2 — mentors keep you going.

🤝

Warm intros

Mentors connect you with people in their network — Partners, Businesses they know, suppliers, etc. Worth the program for these alone.

💪

Encouragement when needed

The first deal is hard. The first downline Partner is hard. A mentor who's been there reminds you it gets easier.
